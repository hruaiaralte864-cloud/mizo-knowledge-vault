# Mizo Academy of Letters (MAL)

**Tags:** #literature #society

The **Mizo Academy of Letters (MAL)**, founded in **1964**, is the apex literary body in Mizoram. Its primary purpose is to promote and standardize the Mizo language and its literature.

## Functions and Awards
The MAL is responsible for:
- Awarding the prestigious **Book of the Year** award, which is the highest literary honour in the state.
- Organizing literary seminars and workshops for young writers.
- Working on Mizo grammar and orthography standardization.

## Significance
During the years of political upheaval, the MAL served as a critical institutional guardian of Mizo culture. It ensured that even as the state was being transformed, its language and literary heritage remained intact and evolving.
