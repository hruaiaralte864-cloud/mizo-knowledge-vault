# Mizo Literature Pioneers



The early development of Mizo literature was driven by individuals who transformed the oral tradition into written form.

## Key Figures
- **Chiauṭera:** Among the first Mizo Christians to write extensive prose and documented hymns.
- **Suakṭluaia:** A key translator who worked with missionaries to adapt Mizo idioms into biblical and educational texts.

These pioneers were the "Generation Zero" of the Mizo literary world, creating the first written materials that established the [[Mizo_Alphabet]] as a tool of the people.
