# K.C. Lalvunga



**K.C. Lalvunga** was a distinguished Mizo diplomat, intellectual, and writer. He served as the Indian Ambassador to several countries and was a deep student of Mizo linguistics and history.

## Intellectual Legacy
He advocated for the preservation of Mizo cultural values through education and literature. His essays are considered essential reading for understanding the philosophical underpinnings of modern Mizo identity.
