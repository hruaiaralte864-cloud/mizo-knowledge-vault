# The Hymns of Rokunga

**Tags:** #literature #religion #music

While [[Rokunga]] is known as the national poet, his hymns hold a sacred place in the Mizo spiritual experience.

## Theme of the Land
His hymns uniquely blend Christian hope with a deep, mystical love for the Mizo hills. In songs like *'Mizo fate u, enga nge in awm?'*, he called for unity and spiritual awakening during times of great social suffering.
