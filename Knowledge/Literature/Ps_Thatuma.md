# Ps. Ṭhaṭuma

**Tags:** #biography #literature #religion

**Ps. Ṭhaṭuma** is one of the most prolific and beloved hymn writers in the Mizo language. His songs are characterized by their deep emotional resonance and their use of classical Mizo imagery to express Christian faith.
