# Mizo Hmeichhe Insuikhawm Pawl (MHIP)

**Tags:** #governance #society #women #NGO

The **MHIP** is the apex organization for women in Mizoram. It works toward the empowerment of women, the protection of their rights, and the preservation of Mizo cultural values through a female perspective.
