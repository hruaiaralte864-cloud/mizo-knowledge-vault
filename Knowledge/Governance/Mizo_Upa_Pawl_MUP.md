# Mizo Upa Pawl (MUP)

**Tags:** #governance #society #elders #NGO

The **MUP** is the association for senior citizens. In Mizo society, elders are deeply respected for their wisdom, and the MUP often acts as an advisory body to both the YMA and the State Government on cultural matters.
