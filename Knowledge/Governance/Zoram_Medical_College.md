# Zoram Medical College (ZMC)

**Tags:** #governance #health #education

**Zoram Medical College (ZMC)**, established in **2018** at Falkawn, is the first medical college in Mizoram.

## Significance
For decades, Mizos had to travel to neighbouring states for medical education. ZMC represents a major milestone in self-sufficiency in healthcare and medical training for the state.
