# Public Works Department (PWD)



The **PWD** in Mizoram faces the monumental task of maintaining roads in one of the world's most landslide-prone regions.

## Engineering Challenges
The state's soil is primarily soft shale and sandstone. Every monsoon season, hundreds of landslides occur. The PWD's work in keeping the "lifeline" highways open is a constant struggle against geography.
