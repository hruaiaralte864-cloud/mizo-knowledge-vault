# Mizoram Peoples Forum (MPF)



The **MPF** is a church-backed organization that plays a critical role in ensuring free and fair elections in Mizoram.

## Impact
It enforces a strict "Code of Ethics" during election cycles, limiting money power and loud campaigning, making Mizoram elections some of the most peaceful in India.
