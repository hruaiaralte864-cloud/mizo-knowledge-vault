# Aizawl Smart City Project



The **Aizawl Smart City Project** is an urban redevelopment initiative focused on modernising the state capital's infrastructure while dealing with its unique hill topography.

## Priorities
- **Traffic Management:** Improving the narrow, steep winding roads.
- **Water Supply:** Solving the chronic water shortage in the hills.
- **Disaster Mitigation:** Landslide prevention and earthquake-resistant building codes.
