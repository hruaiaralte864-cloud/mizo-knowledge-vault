# Mizoram State Planning Board (MSPB)



The **Mizoram State Planning Board (MSPB)** is the apex advisory body responsible for formulating the state's long-term developmental strategy and multi-year plans.

## Role and Function
The MSPB works closely with the Chief Minister's office to align the **[[Mizoram_Socio-Economic_Development_Policy_SEDP]]** with national goals. It conducts the economic surveys that form the basis of the **[[Mizoram_Budget_2025_26_Analysis]]**. Its focus is on transitioning the state from an "import-dependent" economy to a self-sufficient, export-oriented one.

## Strategic Research
The board commissions studies on **[[Flora_of_Mizoram]]** commercialization and sustainable hydro-power. It acts as a bridge between the academic findings of **[[Mizoram_University_MZU]]** and the administrative machinery of the state.

## Future Vision
Recent MSPB papers have highlighted the need for digitized land records (**[[Land_Revenue_Settlement_Mizo]]**) and the creation of "Special Economic Zones" near the Myanmar border to leverage the **[[Kaladan_Multi-Modal_Transit_Transport_Project]]**.
