# Horticulture in Mizoram



Mizoram's climate and hilly terrain make it more naturally suited for **Horticulture** than traditional field agriculture.

## The Growth Sector
The Department of Horticulture focuses on high-value crops that can be grown on slopes:
- **Ginger:** Mizoram is one of India's largest producers of organic ginger.
- **Turmeric:** High-curcumin turmeric is a major export.
- **Fruits:** Oranges, passion fruit, grapes (for the local wine industry), and bananas.
- **Flowers:** Anthuriums from Mizoram are exported globally, including to markets in the UAE and Japan.
