# Mizoram Budget Analysis (2025-2026)



Presented by Chief Minister Pu Lalduhoma on March 4, 2025, the budget for the fiscal year 2025-26 reflects a strategic shift towards "Fiscal Consolidation" and "Handholding" the rural economy.

## Fiscal Summary (Figures in Crore)
| Parameter | 2024-25 (Revised) | 2025-26 (Budget Estimate) | Change (%) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Total Expenditure** | ~14,000 | **15,198** | +8.5% |
| **GSDP Projection** | 48,038 | **36,089** | -24.8% (Conservative Adjustment) |
| **Revenue Surplus** | 565 | **563** | -0.3% |
| **Fiscal Deficit** | ~3.0% GSDP | **2.8% GSDP** | -0.2% (Targeted Decrease) |

## Flagship Scheme: Mizoram Bana (Handholding)
A massive **75% increase** in allocation was granted to the state's flagship scheme, the **[[Mizoram_Socio-Economic_Development_Policy_SEDP]]**.
- **Objective:** Direct financial "handholding" to transition families from subsistence to commercial production.
- **Grant:** ₹50,000 disbursement per beneficiary family in initial phases.
- **Reach:** Targeting over 60,000 households in the first cycle.

## Sectoral Allocations (2025-26)
- **Education:** 16.4% of total budget.
- **Health:** 8.2% of total budget.
- **Rural Development:** 7.5% of total budget.
