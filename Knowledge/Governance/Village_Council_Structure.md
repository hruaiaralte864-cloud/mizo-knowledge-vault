# Village Council Structure

**Tags:** #governance #society

Following the abolition of the chieftainship, the **Village Council (VC)** became the primary governing body at the grassroots level in Mizoram.

## Functions
The VC is an elected body responsible for:
- Allotment of [[Jhum_Cultivation|Jhum land]].
- Sanitation and village hygiene.
- Resolution of local minor disputes.
- Implementation of government welfare schemes.

It is a more democratic evolution of the traditional council of elders that used to advise the chiefs.
