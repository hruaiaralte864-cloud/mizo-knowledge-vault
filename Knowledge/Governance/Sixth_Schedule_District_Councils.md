# Autonomous District Councils (ADCs)



Mizoram has three ADCs under the Sixth Schedule of the Indian Constitution:
1. **[[Lai_Autonomous_District_Council|LADC]]**
2. **[[Mara_Autonomous_District_Council|MADC]]**
3. **[[Chakma_Autonomous_District_Council|CADC]]**

These bodies allow for a degree of self-governance for minority ethnic groups in the southern part of the state.
