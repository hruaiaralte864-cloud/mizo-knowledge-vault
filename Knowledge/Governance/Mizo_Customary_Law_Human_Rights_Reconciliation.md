## 2. Divorce and "Sumchhuah" Logic
Traditional Mizo divorce (*Sumchhuah*) involves a unique logic of **"Marriage Price Restoration."**
- **Customary View**: If a woman leaves a man (*Sumchhuah*), she returns the marriage price (*Man*). If the man sends her away (*Mak*), he loses the price.
- **Human Rights Critique**: Modern research highlights how this economic logic can discourage women from leaving abusive situations.
- **2026 Reconciliation**: The **[[Mizo_Hmeichhe_Insuikhawm_Pawl_MHIP]]** is providing data to the state to reform these terms into a "No-Fault" maintenance system compatible with international rights.
