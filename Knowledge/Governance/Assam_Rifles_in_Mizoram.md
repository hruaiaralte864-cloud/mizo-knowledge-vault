# Assam Rifles in Mizoram

**Tags:** #governance #military #history

The **Assam Rifles** (known as the 'Sentinels of the Northeast') have a long and complex history in Mizoram.

## Frontier Role
Originally established in the 19th century as the 'Cachar Levy', they were the primary military force used by the British to annex and settle the Lushai Hills.

## Modern Relationship
Despite their role in the counter-insurgency years, the Assam Rifles today maintain a close relationship with the Mizo people, often providing medical aid and disaster relief in remote border areas. Their sprawling headquarters in the heart of Aizawl (**Lammual**) is a historical landmark.
