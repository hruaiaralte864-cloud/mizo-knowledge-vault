# Environment, Forest and Climate Change Department



With over **88% of its land covered in forest**, the Environment and Forest Department is one of the most critical government bodies in Mizoram.

## Responsibilities
- Management of Protected Areas like **[[Dampa_Tiger_Reserve]]** and **[[Phawngpui_Blue_Mountain|Phawngpui National Park]]**.
- Regulation of [[Jhum_Cultivation]] cycles to ensure forest regeneration.
- **Bamboo Management:** Oversight of the state's vast bamboo resources — see [[Bamboo_and_Crafts]].
