# Animal Husbandry and Veterinary Department



In a society where **Pork** is the primary protein (see [[Mizo_Cuisine]]), the AH&V Department is vital.

## Responsibilities
- Improving piggery and poultry breeds.
- Management of animal diseases across the porous international border.
- Promotion of dairy farming in the colder northern districts.
