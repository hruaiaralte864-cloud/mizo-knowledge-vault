# Paihte Clan

**Tags:** #history #clan #society

The **Paihte** (or *Paite*) are a major Mizo clan with a strong presence in the eastern parts of Mizoram and the neighboring Chin State of Myanmar.

## Cultural Distinctiveness
The Paihte have a very distinct dialect and traditional attire. Historically, they were known for their sophisticated village defense systems and their trade relationship with groups in the Chindwin valley.

## Integration
While the Paihte maintain a strong sub-identity, they have been integral to the pan-Mizo political movement, particularly during the [[MNF_Uprising_1966]]. The Paihte contribution to the Mizo struggle for autonomy is highly regarded.
