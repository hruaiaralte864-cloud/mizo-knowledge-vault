# United Mizo Freedom Organisation (UMFO)

**Tags:** #history #politics

The **United Mizo Freedom Organisation (UMFO)** was a major political rival to the [[Mizo_Union]] in the late 1940s.

## The "Burma Option"
Unlike the Mizo Union, which favoured integration with India, the UMFO advocated for the Lushai Hills to join **Burma (Myanmar)**. Their reasoning was based on the close ethnic and linguistic ties between the Mizo and the Chin people of Myanmar.

## Legacy
Although the UMFO eventually merged with other groups and the "Burma Option" lost momentum, the party represented a significant portion of Mizo public opinion that desired a larger, pan-Zomi political entity. Their arguments about ethnic contiguity still echo in modern discussions about [[Mizo_Diaspora]] and [[Myanmar_Border_Relations]].
