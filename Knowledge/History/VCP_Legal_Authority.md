﻿# Legal Node: VCP Legal Authority



## 1. Jurisprudential Classification
- **Category**: Governance
- **Authority**: Village Council
- **Core Rule**: Modern successor to the Chieftain's judicial powers.

## 2. Judicial Context
The VCP Legal Authority is a foundational pillar of **[[Mizo_Life_and_Tradition]]**. Unlike Western adversarial law, traditional Mizo justice focuses on **Restorative Harmony**—ensuring that the social fabric of the village remains intact through mediation and symbolic compensation.

## 3. Legal Philosophy
The primary local justice provider in 21st century Mizoram. In the ancient **[[Village_Council_Structure|Chief's Court]]**, the primary goal was the preservation of **[[Tlawmngaihna]]**. A crime was not just an offense against an individual, but a disruption of the community's collective peace.

## 4. Modern Standing
Under the **[[Mizoram_State_Governance_Model]]** and the **[[Modern_Codification_1956|1956 Act]]**, these customary laws are officially recognized by the Indian Constitution (Article 371G).
