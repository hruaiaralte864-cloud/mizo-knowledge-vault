﻿# Clan Node: Tlau Clan History



## 1. Lineage Classification
- **Primary Group**: Tlau
- **Sub-Branches/Families**: Tlau branches
- **Traditional Homeland**: Lunglei District

## 2. Historical Origins
The Tlau Clan History is a distinguished branch of the **[[Zohnahthlak]]** people. Oral traditions suggest their migration routes followed the parent clan movements from the **[[Mizo_Clan_Migrations_Data|Chhinlung]]** sites.

## 3. Clan Characteristics
Known for resilience during clan wars. They are traditionally known for their adherence to the **[[Tlawmngaihna]]** code and their contribution to the **[[Village_Council_Structure]]**.

## 4. Notable Figures
Members of this clan are memorialized in the **[[Biographies_A-Z_Index]]** for their roles as *Pasalthas* and early community leaders.
