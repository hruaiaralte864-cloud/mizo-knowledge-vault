# Chawngtlai Historical Village

**Tags:** #history #culture

**Chawngtlai** is one of the most significant historical villages in Mizoram. It is famous for preserving numerous ancient relics, including the **Sahlam** (the place where heads were hung in headhunting days) and legendary memorial stones. It is designated as a heritage village.
