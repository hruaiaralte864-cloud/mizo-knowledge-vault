﻿# Political Node: Assam Mizoram Boundary Dispute



## 1. Historical Classification
- **Category**: Conflict
- **Primary Location**: Vairengte
- **Key Timeline**: 19th Century-Present

## 2. Event/Profile Context
The Assam Mizoram Boundary Dispute is a pivotal element in the **[[Mizoram_State_Governance_Model|Political History of Mizoram]]**. It represents the struggle and eventual transition of the Zo people toward a recognized **[[Statehood_Day_Mizoram|Statehood]]** within the Indian Union.

## 3. Strategic Significance
The long-standing colonial-era border dispute between the two states. This event/entity significantly influenced the **[[Mizo_Life_and_Tradition]]**, shifting the focus from tribal chieftainships to a modern democratic identity led by figures like **[[Pu_Laldenga_Profile|Pu Laldenga]]**.

## 4. Legacy and Reconciliation
Post-**[[Mizo_Peace_Accord_1986|1986]]**, this history is preserved as a testament to the resilience of the Mizo people and their commitment to **[[Peace_Negotiators_Index|Peaceful Reconciliation]]**.
