# The Historic 1952 Elections



The **1952 District Council Elections** were a watershed moment in Mizo history.

## The Result
The **[[Mizo_Union]]** won a smashing victory, capturing 17 out of 18 seats. This proved that the Mizo people overwhelmingly supported the democratic transition over the old [[Lal_System]]. It gave the Mizo Union the mandate to negotiate the formal abolition of the chieftainship.
