# Fanai Clan



The **Fanai** are a respected clan primarily found in the southern parts of Mizoram.

## Historical Reputation
The Fanai were known as the "Diplomats of the South." They were famous for their ability to mediate disputes between the more aggressive raiding clans of the east and the southern groups.

## Governance
Fanai chiefs were renowned for their wisdom and were often sought after as advisors by the Sailo chiefs of the central hills.
