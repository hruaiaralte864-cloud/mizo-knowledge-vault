# The Sailo Chiefs

**Tags:** #history #clan #governance

The **Sailo** is a lineage within the Lusei clan that produced almost all the major traditional rulers (chiefs) of the Mizo people for over 200 years.

## Rise to Power
The Sailo lineage traces its ancestry to a man named **Sailova**. Through strategic marriages, diplomatic skill, and military dominance, the Sailos displaced other ruling families in the 18th century. By the 19th century, a "Sailo" was essentially synonymous with being a chief.

## Administrative Legacy
The Sailo chiefs perfected the **[[Village_System]]**. They were responsible for:
- Allocating land for [[Jhum_Cultivation]].
- Serving as the supreme judge in village disputes.
- Leading the village in times of war.

Their power was absolute but tempered by the Council of Elders (*Upas*). The Sailo era ended with the [[Chieftainship_Abolition_1954]].
