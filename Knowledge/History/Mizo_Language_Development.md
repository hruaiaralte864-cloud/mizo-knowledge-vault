# Mizo Language Development

**Tags:** #history #language

The **Mizo language** has transitioned from an oral dialect cluster to a standardized, vibrant literary language in less than 150 years.

## Milestone Events
- **1894:** Introduction of the Latin script by [[Rev_JH_Lorrain|missionaries]].
- **1938:** Publication of the first history by [[Rev_Liangkhaia_History|Rev. Liangkhaia]].
- **Modern Era:** Recognition as an official language and its use as a medium of instruction in schools.
