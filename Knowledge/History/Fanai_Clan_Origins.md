## 1. Lineage Classification
- **Primary Group**: Fanai
- **Sub-Branches/Families**: Fanai and related groups
- **Traditional Homeland**: Southern Borders

## 2. Historical Origins
The Fanai Clan Origins is a distinguished branch of the **[[Zohnahthlak]]** people. Oral traditions suggest their migration routes followed the parent clan movements from the **[[Mizo_Clan_Migrations_Data|Chhinlung]]** sites.

## 3. Clan Characteristics
Known for unique cultural nuances. They are traditionally known for their adherence to the **[[Tlawmngaihna]]** code and their contribution to the **[[Village_Council_Structure]]**.

## 4. Notable Figures
Members of this clan are memorialized in the **[[Biographies_A-Z_Index]]** for their roles as *Pasalthas* and early community leaders.
