# The Mizo Union (1946)



The **Mizo Union** was the first political party in the Lushai Hills, founded on **April 9, 1946**. Its formation marked the beginning of modern democratic politics in Mizoram.

## Objectives
The Mizo Union was founded by commoners as a direct challenge to the power of the **[[Lal_System|Sailo Chiefs]]**. Their primary goals were:
1. Abolition of the hereditary chieftainship.
2. Integration with the soon-to-be-independent India.
3. Development of education and infrastructure in the hills.

## Political Victory
The Mizo Union was immensely popular and won the first elections to the District Council in 1952. They successfully pressured the Indian government to pass the **Lushai Hills District (Acquisition of Chiefs' Rights) Act, 1954**, which formally ended the [[Lal_System]].
