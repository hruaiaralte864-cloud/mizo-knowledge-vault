# The Mizo National Front (MNF)



The **Mizo National Front (MNF)** was founded by [[Laldenga]] in 1961, evolving from the **Mizo National Famine Front (MNFF)** formed during the [[Mautam_Famine_1959]].

Its central demand was the creation of a sovereign, independent state of *Greater Mizoram* encompassing all Mizo-inhabited territories across India, Bangladesh, and Myanmar.

## Phases
- **1961–1966:** Political mobilisation and secret military preparation.
- **1966–1986:** Active armed insurgency ([[MNF_Uprising_1966]]).
- **1986–present:** Democratic political party after signing the [[Mizo_Peace_Accord_1986]].

Today the MNF continues as a legitimate political party and has remained one of Mizoram's dominant parties.
