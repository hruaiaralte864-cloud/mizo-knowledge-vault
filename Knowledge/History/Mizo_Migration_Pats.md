# The Mizo Migration Paths



The current Mizo population followed distinct migration paths from their legendary origins in the east (Myanmar and beyond).

## The Three Main Branches
1. **The Lusei-Sailo Branch:** Migrated through the central hills, establishing the dominant settlements in Aizawl and Lunglei.
2. **The Hmar-Paihte Branch:** Moved primarily through the northern and eastern fringes, maintaining closer ties to the Chin hills.
3. **The Southern Branch:** Composed of the Mara and Lai people, who settled in the southernmost tips of the state.

## Strategic Locations
- **Khawbung:** One of the oldest settlement sites where many clans branched out.
- **Tiau River:** The crossing point that defines the "arrival" into the current Mizo hills.
