# Khiangte Clan



The **Khiangte** are an ancient and respected Mizo clan. Historically, they were known as a "silent but strong" group, often allied with the Lusei but maintaining their own ancestral traditions.

## Origin Legends
Khiangte oral tradition claims they were among the first to cross the **Tiau River** during the great migration from the east. They are often associated with the 'Chawngtui' migration branch.

## Social Role
The Khiangte were traditionally respected for their agricultural skill and their prowess in hunting. Many prominent Mizo families today trace their lineage back to Khiangte sub-clans such as **Khupchawng, Khenglawt**, and **Chawngte**.
