# Hmar Clan

**Tags:** #history #clan #society

The **Hmar** people are a major ethnic group within the broader Mizo (Zo) identity. They have a distinct language and a rich history of migration that places them among the earliest inhabitants of the Mizo hills.

## Geographical Distribution
Hmar communities are found across Mizoram, Manipur, and Assam. In Mizoram, they are concentrated primarily in the northern districts. They maintain a very strong cultural and literary tradition, often distinct from the Lusei mainstream.

## Contribution to Identity
The Hmar were among the first to adopt [[Christianity_in_Mizoram|Christianity]] in the northern regions. They have contributed significantly to Mizo literature and music. The Hmar people's insistence on their unique identity while remaining part of the larger Mizo family has helped shape the pluralistic nature of modern Mizoram.
