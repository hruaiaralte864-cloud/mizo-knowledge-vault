# Zoram People's Movement (ZPM)



The **Zoram People's Movement (ZPM)** is the newest major political force in Mizoram, formed in **2017** as a merger of several civic organisations and political groups.

## Formation
The ZPM emerged from deep public frustration with corruption and the long alternating dominance of the [[Mizo_National_Front|MNF]] and Indian National Congress. It presented itself as a clean-slate, anti-corruption, youth-oriented alternative.

## 2023 Election Victory
In the **November 2023 state elections**, the ZPM achieved a stunning upset — winning **27 out of 40 seats** in the Mizoram Legislative Assembly, defeating both the incumbent MNF government under [[Zoramthanga]] and the Congress.

**Lalduhoma**, a former IPS officer and longtime activist, became Chief Minister — the first time Mizoram had a Chief Minister from outside the two traditional parties.
