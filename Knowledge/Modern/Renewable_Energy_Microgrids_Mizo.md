# Renewable Energy: Ridge Microgrids

**Tags:** #energy #modern #nature #sustainability

Mizoram is aggressively expanding its renewable energy footprint to achieve the "Net Zero" goals of the **[[Mizoram_2050_Projections]]**.

## Decentalized Solar
While large-scale solar farms are difficult on steep ridges, the state is leading in **Rooftop Solar and Micro-grids**. Villages along the Indo-Myanmar border now use localized solar grids to power schools and community halls, reducing dependence on the centralized grid which is often disrupted by storms.

## Micro-Hydel Projects
The state's numerous hill streams are being utilized for "Micro-Hydel" power generation. These low-impact projects provide continuous energy for the **[[Mizoram_Bamboo_Policy_Industrialization]]** units without the ecological disruption of large dams.

## Policy Support
The **[[Mizoram_State_Planning_Board]]** provides significant subsidies for private solar adoption, particularly for the **[[Startup_Ecosystem_Aizawl]]** tech hubs. This energy independence is a key pillar of the **[[Mizoram_Socio-Economic_Development_Policy_SEDP]]**.
