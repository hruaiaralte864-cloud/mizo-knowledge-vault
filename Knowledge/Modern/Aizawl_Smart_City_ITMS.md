# Aizawl Smart City: ITMS and Vertical Solutions



The **Aizawl Smart City** project is a multi-billion rupee initiative to revitalize the capital city using digital infrastructure and high-tech urban design.

## ITMS: Intelligent Traffic Management
Aizawl is famous for its "Zero-Honking" and "Queue-based" traffic culture. The **Intelligent Traffic Management System (ITMS)** enhances this by:
- Deploying AI-powered cameras to monitor ridge-junctions.
- Real-time traffic flow analysis to adjust signals based on peak-hour "up-slope" demand.
- Digital signage that updates drivers on landslide-affected routes in the **[[Geography_Overview]]**.

## Vertical Parking: Mechanical Solutions
Due to zero horizontal space on the ridges, the Smart City mission has introduced **Mechanical Vertical Parking**. These automated towers allow dozens of cars to be parked in the footprint of a single vehicle, alleviating the chronic parking shortage in the Millennium Center and Treasury Square zones of the **[[Aizawl_District]]**.

## Citizen Engagement
The "Aizawl Smart City App" allows citizens to report civic issues directly to the authorities, integrating with the **[[Mizoram_Loka_SEWA]]** portal for transparent governance.
