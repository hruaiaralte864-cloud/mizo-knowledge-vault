# Institutional Node: Mizoram State Museum

**Tags:** #governance #education #medical #institutions #Mizoram #research #Aizawl

## 1. Statutory Classification
- **Category**: Cultural
- **Headquarters**: Aizawl
- **Established**: 1977

## 2. Institutional Role
The Mizoram State Museum is a cornerstone of **[[Mizoram_State_Governance_Model|Modern Mizo Society]]**. Whether in **Cultural** or public service, it has played a critical role in transitioning the state from the **[[Chieftainship_Era_Manual|Chieftainship Era]]** to a modern democratic entity.

## 3. Historical Impact
Preserving the artifacts and history of the Zo people. Since its establishment in **1977**, it has been instrumental in the development of **[[Aizawl_City_Data|Aizawl]]** as the administrative and cultural hub of the Zo people.

## 4. Modern Connectivity
Under the **[[Greater_Mizo_Infrastructure_2026]]** master plan, it is now digitally integrated with the **[[Mizoram_State_e-Governance_Portal]]**, ensuring transparent service delivery to all **[[District_Registry_Mizoram|Districts]]**.
