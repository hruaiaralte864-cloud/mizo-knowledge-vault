﻿# Institutional Node: RIPANS Aizawl



## 1. Statutory Classification
- **Category**: Medical
- **Headquarters**: Zemabawk
- **Established**: 1995

## 2. Institutional Role
The RIPANS Aizawl is a cornerstone of **[[Mizoram_State_Governance_Model|Modern Mizo Society]]**. Whether in **Medical** or public service, it has played a critical role in transitioning the state from the **[[Chieftainship_Era_Manual|Chieftainship Era]]** to a modern democratic entity.

## 3. Historical Impact
Regional Institute of Paramedical and Nursing Sciences. Since its establishment in **1995**, it has been instrumental in the development of **[[Aizawl_City_Data|Aizawl]]** as the administrative and cultural hub of the Zo people.

## 4. Modern Connectivity
Under the **[[Greater_Mizo_Infrastructure_2026]]** master plan, it is now digitally integrated with the **[[Mizoram_State_e-Governance_Portal]]**, ensuring transparent service delivery to all **[[District_Registry_Mizoram|Districts]]**.
