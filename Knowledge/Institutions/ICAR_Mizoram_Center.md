# ICAR Mizoram Centre (Kolosib)



The **Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) Mizoram Centre**, located near Kolasib, is the premier scientific body dedicated to hill agriculture in the state.

## Research Focus
The center focuses on finding high-yield varieties of crops that can thrive in the steep terrain and high rainfall of Mizoram. It was instrumental in the scientific validation of **[[Mizo_Chilly_Mizo_Chay]]** and has conducted extensive research on **[[Mizo_Cardamom_Agriculture]]** and acid soil management.

## Sustainable Jhum Models
ICAR works closely with scientists like **[[Dr_Lianzela_Sustainable_Farming]]** to design sustainable alternatives to traditional shifting cultivation. Their "Integrated Farming Systems" (IFS) models are used as a template for government schemes like the **[[Mizoram_Socio-Economic_Development_Policy_SEDP]]**.

## Training and Outreach
The center regularly hosts training sessions for local farmers, providing them with technical knowledge on pest control and water harvesting. Its location in the **[[Kolasib_District]]** allows it to serve as a hub for the agricultural belt of northern Mizoram.
