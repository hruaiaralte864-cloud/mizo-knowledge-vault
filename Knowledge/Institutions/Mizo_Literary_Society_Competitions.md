# Mizo Literary Society (MLS) Competitions

**Tags:** #institutions #literature #arts

The **Mizo Literary Society (MLS)** is a vibrant organization dedicated to the promotion of modern Mizo literature through competitive writing and critical analysis.

## Annual Competitions
The MLS is best known for its annual "Creative Writing" and "Poetry Slams" which attract thousands of participants. These competitions are a major feeder for the **[[Mizo_Academy_of_Letters_MAL]]** and have launched the careers of many contemporary writers.

## Language Preservation
The society focuses on the "purity" of the Mizo language, encouraging writers to use authentic idioms and traditional metaphors. Their competitions often have specific themes relating to **[[Tlawmngaihna]]** and Mizo folklore, ensuring that modern literature stays connected to its roots.

## Publication and Outreach
Leading entries from MLS competitions are published in their monthly journal, providing a platform for marginalized voices and young students. The society also hosts the **[[Zo_Literary_Festival]]**, a major event celebrating the shared heritage of the Zohnahthlak people.
