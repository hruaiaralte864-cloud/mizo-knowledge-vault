# Mizoram Youth Commission (MYC)

**Tags:** #institutions #education #employment

The **Mizoram Youth Commission (MYC)** is a statutory body established by the Government of Mizoram to empower the youth through skill development, carrier counseling, and competitive exam coaching.

## Skill Development and Coaching
The MYC is famous for its "Super 40" and specialized coaching programs for civil services (UPSC/MPSC). It provides scholarships for students studying in top-tier coaching centers across India. Under the current **[[Mizoram_Budget_2025_26_Analysis]]**, the MYC has seen increased funding to expand its "Livelihood Generation" programs, moving beyond just government jobs into technical and vocational training.

## Promoting Local Talent
The commission also organizes "Mizo Idol" and other cultural competitions to provide a platform for the state's artistic youth. It works closely with the **[[Society_of_Fine_Arts_Awards]]** to identify and support young visual artists.

## Employment Exchange
MYC serves as a bridges between the youth and private sector employers. It maintains a database of skilled Mizo youth and holds regular "Job Fairs" in the **[[Aizawl_District]]** to facilitate placement in national and international companies.
