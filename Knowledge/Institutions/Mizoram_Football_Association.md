# Institutional Node: Mizoram Football Association

**Tags:** #governance #education #medical #institutions #Mizoram #research #Aizawl

## 1. Statutory Classification
- **Category**: Sports
- **Headquarters**: Aizawl
- **Established**: 1971

## 2. Institutional Role
The Mizoram Football Association is a cornerstone of **[[Mizoram_State_Governance_Model|Modern Mizo Society]]**. Whether in **Sports** or public service, it has played a critical role in transitioning the state from the **[[Chieftainship_Era_Manual|Chieftainship Era]]** to a modern democratic entity.

## 3. Historical Impact
Governing body of the most popular sport in the state. Since its establishment in **1971**, it has been instrumental in the development of **[[Aizawl_City_Data|Aizawl]]** as the administrative and cultural hub of the Zo people.

## 4. Modern Connectivity
Under the **[[Greater_Mizo_Infrastructure_2026]]** master plan, it is now digitally integrated with the **[[Mizoram_State_e-Governance_Portal]]**, ensuring transparent service delivery to all **[[District_Registry_Mizoram|Districts]]**.
