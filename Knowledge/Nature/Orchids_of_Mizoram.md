# Orchids of Mizoram



Mizoram is home to over **300 species of orchids**, many of which are rare and endemic to the state's high-altitude forests.

## Key Species
- **Vanda coerulea:** The famous Blue Orchid.
- **Dendrobium densiflorum:** Known for its spectacular yellow clusters.
- **Renanthera imschootiana:** A bright red orchid highly prized in the international market.

Mizoram's diverse climate zones allow for a staggering variety of these plants to thrive from the river valleys to the mountain peaks.
