# Foxtail Orchid (Rhynchostylis retusa)

**Mizo Name:** Nauban-hmabak



The **Foxtail Orchid** is widely distributed across the state. It is famous for its long, drooping cylindrical inflorescence that resembles a fox's tail. It bloom during the pre-monsoon season and is a common sight in traditional Mizo gardens.
