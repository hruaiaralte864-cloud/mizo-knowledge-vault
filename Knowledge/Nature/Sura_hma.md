﻿# Ethnobotany: Sura hma



## 1. Botanical Identity
- **Mizo Name**: Sura hma

- **Family**: [[Mizoram_Flora_Taxonomy]]

- **Active Principles**: Blood circulation
- **Traditional Use**: Red fruit used in traditional blood tonic.

Primarily found in the **Sura** ecological zones of Mizoram, often cultivated in household gardens or found wild in the **[[Hmuifang_Forest_Reserve]]**.
