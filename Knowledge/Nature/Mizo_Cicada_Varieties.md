# Mizo Cicadas (Re-thei)



Mizoram has a massive diversity of cicadas. Their rhythmic drone is the defining soundtrack of the Mizo monsoon. Different species carry different names (e.g., *Re-thei-pui*) and their sudden emergence in 17-year or 13-year cycles is noted by village elders.
