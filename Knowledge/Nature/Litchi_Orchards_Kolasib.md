# Litchi Orchards of Kolasib: The Sweet Economy



Kolasib is renowned as the "Fruit Bowl" of Mizoram, and its **Litchi Orchards** are the crown jewel of its horticultural sector.

## Ideal Growing Conditions
The **[[Kolasib_District]]** provides the perfect combination of humid tropical climate and slightly acidic, well-drained soil required for high-quality litchi production. The "Muzaffarpur" and "Shahi" varieties introduced here have adapted exceptionally well, producing fruits known for their sweetness and small seeds.

## Economic Impact
The litchi harvest in May-June is a major economic event. Under the **[[Mizoram_Socio-Economic_Development_Policy_SEDP]]**, the state provides specialized "Mobile Collection Centers" to transport the perishable crops to markets in Silchar and Guwahati. The **[[Mizoram_Rural_Bank]]** offers seasonal credit to orchard owners for pest management and packaging.

## Future: Processing and Export
The **[[Kolasib_Fruit_Canning]]** factory is being upgraded to process litchi juice and pulp, reducing post-harvest losses. This is a key part of the **[[Mizoram_State_Planning_Board]]** vision to turn Kolasib into an export-oriented horticultural hub for Southeast Asia.
