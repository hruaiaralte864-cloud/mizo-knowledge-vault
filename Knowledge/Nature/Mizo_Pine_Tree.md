# Pinus kesiya (Khasi Pine)

**Mizo Name:** Far



The **Khasi Pine** is the dominant tree species in the higher elevations of eastern Mizoram, particularly in the **[[Champhai_District|Champhai]]** and **[[Saitual_District|Saitual]]** districts.

## Significance
- **Timber**: Used extensively in traditional and modern building construction.
- **Ecological**: The pine forests of Champhai create a unique microclimate and support a specific range of undergrowth flora.
