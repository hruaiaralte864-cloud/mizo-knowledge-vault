# Dampa Tiger Reserve

**Tags:** #geography #wildlife #conservation

**Dampa** is the largest wildlife sanctuary in Mizoram, located in the Mamit district bordering Bangladesh.

## Biodiversity
While named for tigers, it is more famous today for its high density of **[[Clouded_Leopard_Mizo|Clouded Leopards]]**, elephants, and over 15 species of primates. It is a critical habitat for Western Hoolock Gibbons.
