# Mizo Oak (Quercus spp.)

**Mizo Name:** Khawlum / Khawthli



Several species of Oak are native to the Mizo hills. They are valued for their incredibly hard wood, used for making agricultural tools and durable house posts. The acorns were traditionally used as supplemental feed for pigs.
