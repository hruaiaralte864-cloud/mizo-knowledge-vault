# Blue Vanda (Vanda coerulea)

**Mizo Name:** Nauban



The **Blue Vanda** is one of the most iconic and rare orchids found in the wild forests of Mizoram.

## Characteristics
Known for its striking bluish-purple flowers, this orchid is highly prized by collectors worldwide. In Mizoram, it grows as an epiphyte on the branches of large hardwood trees.

## Protection Status
Due to over-collection and habitat loss, it is now a protected species under Indian wildlife laws. Several community-run orchidaries in villages like **[[Vaphai_Village|Vaphai]]** and **[[Reiek_Peak|Reiek]]** are working to conserve this species.
