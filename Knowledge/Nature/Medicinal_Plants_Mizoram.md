# Medicinal Plants of Mizoram (Ethnobotany)

**Tags:** #nature #health #knowledge

Mizo traditional medicine relied heavily on the vast biodiversity of the hills.

## Important Plants
- **Lengser:** A ginger-like root used for stomach ailments.
- **Hnathei:** Used for treating skin infections and wounds.
- **Ram-hmarcha:** Wild chilies used for stimulating circulation.

Modern research is now looking into these plants for potential pharmacological applications.
