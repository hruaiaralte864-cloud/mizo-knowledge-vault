# Clouded Leopard (Neofelis nebulosa)

**Mizo Name:** Kel-ral



The **Clouded Leopard** is the apex predator of the Mizo jungles. Extremely elusive and largely arboreal, it is known for its beautiful cloud-like coat patterns. **[[Dampa_Tiger_Reserve]]** is one of the few places in India with a significant population of this rare feline.
