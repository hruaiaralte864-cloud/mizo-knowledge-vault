# Barking Deer (Muntiacus muntjak)

**Mizo Name:** Sakah



The **Barking Deer** is one of the most common large mammals seen in the Mizo forests. Its distinctive call, which sounds like a dog's bark, can be heard across the valleys at dawn and dusk. It is a shy, solitary animal and is a frequent subject of Mizo hunting lore.
