# Wild Boar (Sanghal)

**Tags:** #fauna #wildlife

Wild boars are found throughout the state and are often seen as a threat to **[[Jhum_Cultivation|Jhum]]** crops. They are a primary source of meat for hunters and are respected for their ferocity and strength.
