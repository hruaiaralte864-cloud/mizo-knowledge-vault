# Burmese Python (Rulpui)

**Tags:** #fauna #wildlife

The largest snake found in the Mizo hills. Often found near water sources in the lower valleys. In folklore, giant snakes (*Rulpui*) were often portrayed as guardians of secret treasures or gateways to other realms.
