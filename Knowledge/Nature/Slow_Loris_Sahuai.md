# Slow Loris (Nycticebus bengalensis)

**Mizo Name:** Sahuai



The **Slow Loris** is a nocturnal primate found throughout Mizoram.

## Folklore
In Mizo belief, the *Sahuai* is often associated with the spirit world. Its large, soulful eyes and slow movements make it a figure of mystery. Traditionally, it was considered bad luck to harm a Sahuai near a village site.
