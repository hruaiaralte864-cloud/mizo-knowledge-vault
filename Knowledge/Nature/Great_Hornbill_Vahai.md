# Great Hornbill (Buceros bicornis)

**Mizo Name:** Vahai



The **Great Hornbill** is significantly present in Mizo oral history and songs. Its massive size and loud prehistoric honks make it impossible to miss. In ancient times, its feathers were used in ceremonial headgears for warriors who had achieved great feats.
