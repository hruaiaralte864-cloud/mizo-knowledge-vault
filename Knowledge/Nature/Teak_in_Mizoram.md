# Teak (Tectona grandis)

**Mizo Name:** Thingpui



Teak is one of the most valuable timber trees grown in the lower valleys of western and southern Mizoram. Large-scale teak plantations were introduced by the Forest Department and have become a major source of revenue for many Mizo families.
