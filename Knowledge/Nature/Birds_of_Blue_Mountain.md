# Birds of Phawngpui (Blue Mountain)



The **Phawngpui National Park** is a paradise for birdwatchers.

## Rare Species
- **Blyth's Tragopan:** A spectacularly colored pheasant.
- **Mrs. Hume's Pheasant:** The state bird (Vavu).
- **Great Indian Hornbill:** A majestic bird with a distinct call.
