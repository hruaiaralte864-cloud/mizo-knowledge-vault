# Saza (Mizo Millet)

**Tags:** #flora #agriculture

A traditional cereal grain that was once a staple of the Mizo diet before the dominance of white rice. It is hardy and grows well on steep slopes. Recently, it has seen a revival as a healthy, traditional food alternative.
