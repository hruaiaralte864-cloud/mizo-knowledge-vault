# Ngatin (Freshwater Mahseer)

**Tags:** #fauna #fish

The **Chocolate Mahseer** is a prized game and food fish in the rivers like **[[Tlawng_River|Tlawng]]** and **[[Kolodyne_River|Chhimtuipui]]**. It is a symbol of the health of Mizoram's river systems.
