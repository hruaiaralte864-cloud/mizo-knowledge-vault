# Mrs. Hume's Pheasant (Syrmaticus humiae)

**Mizo Name:** Vavu

**Tags:** #fauna #birds #state_symbol

This stunningly beautiful bird is the **State Bird of Mizoram**. It is found in the open pine forests of eastern Mizoram. It is known for its metallic blue and chestnut feathers. It is an endangered species and is the subject of intense conservation efforts.
