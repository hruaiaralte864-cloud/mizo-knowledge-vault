# Zo-zial (Traditional Tobacco)

**Tags:** #flora #culture

Mizo tobacco is a strong, local variety grown in small patches. It was traditionally smoked in bamboo pipes or hand-rolled into idiosyncratic 'Zo-zial' cigarettes. It played a major role in social interactions between elders.
