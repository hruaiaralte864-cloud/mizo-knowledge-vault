# Himalayan Serow (Capricornis thar)

**Mizo Name:** Saza

**Tags:** #fauna #wildlife #state_symbol

The **Himalayan Serow** is the **State Animal of Mizoram**.

## Characteristics
It is a goat-antelope with a thick coat, large ears, and short, slightly curved horns. In Mizoram, it is found in steep, rocky, and forested terrains at altitudes ranging from 500 to 2,500 meters.

## Cultural Significance
The *Saza* is admired in Mizo culture for its agility and its ability to navigate impossible cliffs. It is often a symbol of grit and reclusiveness.
