# Hnim hna (Traditional Medicinal Plants)



**Hnim hna** refers to the vast array of plants and herbs used in traditional Mizo healing. Before the advent of modern hospitals, Mizo 'Puithiam' and herbalists relied on these for everything from treating wounds to curing internal ailments.
