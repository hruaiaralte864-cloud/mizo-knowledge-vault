# Ethnobotany: Tui par

**Tags:** #science #nature #botany #medicine #HnimHna #research

## 1. Botanical Identity
- **Mizo Name**: Tui par
- **Scientific Name**: *Nymphaea pubescens*
- **Family**: [[Mizoram_Flora_Taxonomy]]

## 2. Pharmaceutical Properties
- **Active Principles**: Cooling, Skin tone
- **Traditional Use**: Water lily root used in traditional skin care.

## 3. Ecological Niche
Primarily found in the **Tui** ecological zones of Mizoram, often cultivated in household gardens or found wild in the **[[Hmuifang_Forest_Reserve]]**.

## 4. Preservation Status
Listed in the **[[Mizo_Life_and_Tradition]]** pharmacopoeia. Modern extraction research is ongoing at the **[[Mizoram_Information_Technology_Policy|Zoram Bio-Tech labs]]**.
