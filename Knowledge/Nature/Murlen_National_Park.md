# Murlen National Park

**Tags:** #geography #wildlife

Located in the Champhai district, **Murlen** is often compared to the Amazon rainforest due to its incredibly thick canopy. It is a prime habitat for the **[[Mrs_Humes_Pheasant|Vavu]]** and several rare cat species.
