# Modern Mizo Music Scene



Mizoram has one of the most vibrant and distinct modern music scenes of any small state in India.

## Gospel and Church Music
The foundation of Mizo musical culture today is **gospel and Christian hymnody**. The Mizo church choir tradition produces extraordinarily high levels of musical literacy — four-part harmony singing in church is near-universal. This choral tradition creates a population saturated in musical skill.

## Mizo Rock and Pop
Building on this musical base, Mizoram has developed a thriving rock, indie, and pop scene:
- Dozens of original Mizo-language rock bands perform original music.
- Annual music festivals like **Zofest** attract thousands.

## Mizo Idol
Modelled on the *American Idol* format, **Mizo Idol** is among the most-watched television events in Mizoram, producing a string of recording artists who perform exclusively in the Mizo language.

## Filmmizo
A small but growing Mizo-language film industry (nicknamed *Filmmizo*) produces both serious dramas and comedies, drawing inspiration from both Mizo folklore and contemporary life.
