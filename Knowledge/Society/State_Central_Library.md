# State Central Library (Aizawl)

**Tags:** #society #education #literature

The **State Central Library** in Aizawl is the repository of the Mizo literary soul.

## Collection
It holds thousands of Mizo-language manuscripts, missionary records, and rare books from the colonial era. It serves as a vital research center for scholars of Northeast Indian history and Tibeto-Burman linguistics.
