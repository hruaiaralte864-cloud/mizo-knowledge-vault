# Pachhunga University College (PUC)



**Pachhunga University College (PUC)**, established in **1958**, is the oldest and most prestigious college in Mizoram.

## History
Founded as Aizawl College, it was renamed in honour of **Mr. Pachhunga**, a prominent local businessman who donated the land. It served as the intellectual cradle for the generation of Mizo leaders who negotiated statehood.

## Academic Excellence
PUC is the only constituent college of **[[Mizoram_University]]** and is consistently ranked as one of the best colleges in Northeast India. Its sprawling green campus in Aizawl is a major local landmark.
