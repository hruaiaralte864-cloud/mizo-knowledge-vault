# The Council of Elders (Upas)

**Tags:** #society #history #governance

Traditionally, no Mizo chief (**[[Lal_System|Lal]]**) could rule alone. Every village had a **Council of Elders (Upas)** chosen by the chief for their wisdom and experience.

## Role
They advised the chief on:
- War and peace treaties.
- Solving inter-family disputes.
- Allocation of [[Jhum_Cultivation|Jhum land]].

This tradition of collaborative governance was the ancestor of the modern [[Village_Council_Structure]].
