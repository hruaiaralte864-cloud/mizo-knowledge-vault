# Mizoram Premier League (MPL)



The **Mizoram Premier League (MPL)** is the top-tier professional football league in Mizoram.

## Impact on Indian Football
Since its inception, the MPL has transformed Mizoram into a powerhouse of Indian football. It provides a platform for local players to be scouted by national clubs. The league's success is credited with the rise of **[[Aizawl_FC]]** and dozens of Mizo players in the Indian national team.
