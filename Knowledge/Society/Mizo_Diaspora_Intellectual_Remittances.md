# The Mizo Diaspora: Intellectual and Social Remittances



The **Mizo Diaspora**, spread across the USA, UK, Israel, and Southeast Asia, is a critical engine for the state's modern development. Unlike many diasporas that only send money, the Mizo diaspora focuses on "Intellectual and Social Remittances."

## Strategic Funding
Mizo families abroad are known for funding schools, hospitals, and community halls in their ancestral villages. This is a global expansion of the **[[Tlawmngaihna]]** code. For example, several high-end medical facilities in the **[[Aizawl_District]]** were born from diaspora initiatives.

## Knowledge Transfer
Expatriate Mizo scholars and tech professionals are the primary mentors for the **[[Startup_Ecosystem_Aizawl]]**. They provide the "Silicon Valley" or "Tel Aviv" connections that allow local Mizo startups to access global markets. This is a core part of the **[[Mizoram_Youth_Commission_MYC]]** mentorship program.

## Cultural Ambassadors
The diaspora ensures that the **[[Mizo_Cultural_Identity]]** remains global. Their "Virtual Hnatlang" groups coordinate social events and disaster relief (e.g., during the **[[The_Haupu_Landslide_Disaster]]**) from across the globe, ensuring that the "Mizo Soul" is not bounded by geography.
