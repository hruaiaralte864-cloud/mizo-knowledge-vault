# Mizo Centenarians: The Longevity of the Ridges



Mizoram is home to a high density of centenarians, with many individuals living active lives beyond the age of 100.

## The Factors of Longevity
1. **Vertical Living**: The sheer exertion of climbing steep slopes daily acts as a form of high-intensity functional training, strengthening the cardiovascular system of the elderly.
2. **The Stew Diet**: The low-oil, high-fiber, and umami-rich diet of **[[Mizo_Cuisine_The_Logic_of_Bai]]** provides a steady intake of phytochemicals and fermented nutrients from **[[Mizo_Fermentation_Science_Bekang_Saum]]**.
3. **Social Cohesion**: The **[[Mizo_Philosophy_of_Living]]** (Tlawmngaihna) ensures that the elderly are never isolated but remain integrated into village life.

## Medical Status
Geriatric studies at **[[Civil_Hospital_Aizawl]]** focus on the "Hill-Active" heart, which often shows lower rates of lifestyle diseases compared to urban plains populations.
