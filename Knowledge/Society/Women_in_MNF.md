# Women in the MNF Resistance



During the insurgency years (1966–1986), Mizo women played a silent but critical role in the resistance.

## Roles
While few were combatants, thousands served as intelligence couriers, nurses in jungle camps, and providers of logistics. They bore the brunt of the "village grouping" hardships, and their resilience was vital to the survival of the Mizo community spirit during the conflict.
