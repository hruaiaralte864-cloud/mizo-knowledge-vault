# Mizo Media History



Mizoram has a high newspaper density, a direct reflection of its [[Mizoram_Literacy_and_Education|high literacy rate]].

## Early Newspapers
- **Mizo Chanchin Laishu:** One of the earliest news bulletins published by missionaries.
- **Zoram Thupuan:** Among the first independent Mizo newspapers.

Today, Aizawl produces dozens of daily newspapers in the Mizo language, serving as the primary source of information and public discourse in the state.
