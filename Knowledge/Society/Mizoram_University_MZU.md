# Mizoram University (MZU)



**Mizoram University (MZU)** is a central university established by an Act of Parliament in **2001**.

## Campus
Located at **Tanhril**, on the outskirts of Aizawl, the university campus is known for its architectural blend with the natural forest environment.

## Impact
MZU has been instrumental in providing higher education and research opportunities within the state, reducing the need for students to migrate to other parts of India for advanced degrees. It offers a wide range of courses from tribal studies to modern biotechnology.
