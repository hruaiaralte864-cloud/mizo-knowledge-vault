# Mizo Adoption Customs (Saphun)

**Tags:** #society #law #culture

Traditional Mizo society had a unique form of adoption called **Saphun**.

## The Practice
When an individual or a family joined a new clan, they underwent a ritual of *Saphun*. This meant they were legally and spiritually integrated into the new clan, gaining all rights of inheritance and social protection as if they were born into it.

## Modern Legacy
This tradition contributed to the inclusive nature of Mizo identity, allowing various smaller groups to be absorbed into a larger unified community over centuries.
