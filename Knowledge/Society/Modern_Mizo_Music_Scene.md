# The Modern Mizo Music Scene



Mizoram is often called the "Music Capital of India."

## Genre Diversity
From the heavy metal and rock scenes in Aizawl to the soulful choir music in churches, music is a universal language in Mizoram. The state has produced numerous nationally acclaimed artists and continues to host major music festivals.
