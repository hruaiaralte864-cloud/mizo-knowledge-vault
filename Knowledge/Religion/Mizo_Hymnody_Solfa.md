# Mizo Hymnody and Tonic Solfa

**Tags:** #religion #music #linguistics

Mizoram is one of the few places in the world where **Tonic Solfa** (d-r-m-f-s-l-t-d) remains the primary method of musical instruction and performance in churches. This legacy was introduced by Welsh missionaries and has defined Mizo choral culture.
