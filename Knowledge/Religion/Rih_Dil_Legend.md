# Rih Dil — The Sacred Lake of the Dead



**Rih Dil** (also known as Rih Lake) is a heart-shaped, extraordinarily clear lake located just inside the border of modern-day Myanmar's Chin State, near the [[Champhai_District]] border.

In traditional Mizo cosmology, Rih Dil was the mandatory waypoint for every soul on its journey to the afterlife. Regardless of where a Mizo person died — whether in a distant jungle or a faraway land — their spirit first travelled to Rih Dil.

The lake's perfectly heart-shaped outline, its unusual clarity, and the fact that it is unusually bottomless (no anchor-line has ever reached the bottom) all contributed to its sacred mystique. Standing at its edge at dusk, the Mizo people said one could sometimes hear the whispers of departing souls.
