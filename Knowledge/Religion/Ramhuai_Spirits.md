# Ramhuai — Mizo Evil Spirits

**Tags:** #religion #folklore

**Ramhuai** (*Ram* = the wilderness/jungle, *Huai* = spirit) are the evil spirits of traditional Mizo animism. They inhabit specific physical locations:

- **Tui huai:** Water spirits in rivers and pools — cause drowning and water-related illness.
- **Thing huai:** Tree spirits inside large, ancient trees — cause fever if a tree is carelessly felled.
- **Beng huai:** Cave spirits.
- **Mual huai:** Hillside spirits at specific outcropping rocks.

Animals acting strangely, sudden illness, or unexplained misfortune were all attributed to Ramhuai interference. The [[Pre-Christian_Animism|Bawlpu (shaman)]] was called to identify which specific Ramhuai had been offended and to prescribe appropriate sacrifice.
