# Mitthi Khua — The Village of the Dead



**Mitthi Khua** (*Mitthi* = dead, *Khua* = village) is the primary afterlife destination in traditional Mizo cosmology — more purgatorial waiting-place than paradise.

Every Mizo soul, upon death, was believed to undertake a long journey to the sacred lake **[[Rih_Dil_Legend|Rih Dil]]** in what is now Myanmar. After passing through the lake, they arrived at Mitthi Khua — a grey, shadowy mirror of the earthly village where they lived out a second, diminished existence.

In Mitthi Khua, the dead continued farming, hunting, and interacting — but with less vitality than life. The exceptional few who had earned [[Thangchhuah]] status bypassed Mitthi Khua and went directly to [[Pialral]] (paradise).
