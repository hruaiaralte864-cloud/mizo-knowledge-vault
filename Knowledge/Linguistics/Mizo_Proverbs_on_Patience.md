# Proverbs on Patience (Dawhtheihna)



A collection of proverbs emphasizing that "The slow walker sees the scenery" (*Muangchana'n hmuhnom a hmu ngun*). This reflects the Mizo survival strategy in the demanding terrain of the hills.
