# Chhura Inbuantuar



**Literal Meaning:** Chhura's wrestling match.

**Usage:** Refers to a task or conflict that is interminable, fruitless, or where the participants keep repeating the same mistakes. Based on the comic failures of the folk hero **[[Chhura]]**.
