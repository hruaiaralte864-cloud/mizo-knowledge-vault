# Sapui Nihngeih

**Tags:** #linguistics #proverbs

**Literal Meaning:** Making a blood-brotherhood with a tiger.

**Cautionary Wisdom:** Used to warn against making alliances with dangerous, untrustworthy, or powerful people who might turn on you. It emphasizes the need for wisdom in choosing one's associates.
