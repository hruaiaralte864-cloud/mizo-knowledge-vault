# Linguistic Roots of Tlawmngaihna



An analysis of the word 'Tlawmngaihna'. It is a compound of 'Tlawm' (to be defeated/to yield) and 'Ngai' (to consider/to value). Linguistically, it implies "valuing the state of being self-sacrificing or humble."
