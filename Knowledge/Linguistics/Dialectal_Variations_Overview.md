# Dialectal Variations: Lusei vs. Others

**Tags:** #linguistics #history

While 'Duhlian' (Lusei) is the official state language, several other dialects like Hmar, Lai, and Mara preserve unique linguistic structures that offer clues about the separate migration paths of different Mizo tribes.
