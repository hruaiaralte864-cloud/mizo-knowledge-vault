# Mizo Honorifics and Addresses



Mizo social structure is reflected in how people are addressed.

## Terms
- **Pu**: Mr. / Respected Elder (Male)
- **Pi**: Ms. / Respected Elder (Female)
- **Ka pa / Ka nu**: My father / My mother (often used for non-relatives as a sign of respect).
- **U**: Short for 'Upa' (Elder sibling/Respected senior).
