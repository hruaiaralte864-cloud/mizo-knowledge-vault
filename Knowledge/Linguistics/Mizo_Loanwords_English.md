# English Loanwords in Mizo



Since the arrival of missionaries, hundreds of English words have been adapted into Mizo phonology.

## Examples
- **Sikul**: School
- **Khawk**: Chalk
- **Moter**: Motor/Car
- **Sapi**: Sahib (referring to British officers)
