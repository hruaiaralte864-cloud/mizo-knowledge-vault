# Mizo Thufing (Proverbs)



**Mizo Thufing** represents the distilled wisdom of generations of ancestors. These proverbs are often rooted in the agrarian lifestyle, hunting experiences, and the high moral code of **[[Tlawmngaihna]]**.

## Common Themes
- **Community**: The importance of unity.
- **Diligence**: Hard work in the fields.
- **Honor**: Maintaining one's integrity (*Hmingchhia*).
