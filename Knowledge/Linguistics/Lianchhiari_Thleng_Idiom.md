# Lianchhiari Thleng

**Tags:** #linguistics #idioms #folklore

**Literal Meaning:** Lianchhiari's plate.

**Usage:** Refers to something that is exceptionally clean or something that has been completely utilized with nothing wasted. Rooted in the legend of **[[Lianchhiari]]** and her meticulous nature.
