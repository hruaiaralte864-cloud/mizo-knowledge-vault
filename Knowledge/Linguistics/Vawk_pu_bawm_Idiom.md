# Vawk pu bawm



**Literal Meaning:** A pig-rearer's basket.

**Usage:** Describes a messy, unorganized, or cluttered collection of items. Since traditional pig farming involved carrying various scraps and tools in a large, loosely packed basket, the image represents chaos.
