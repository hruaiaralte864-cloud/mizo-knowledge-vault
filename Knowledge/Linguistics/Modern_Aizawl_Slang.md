# Modern Aizawl Slang

**Tags:** #linguistics #modern

The youth of Aizawl have developed a distinctive street slang that blends English, Korean (due to K-pop influence), and traditional Mizo roots.

## Common Terms
- **'Aia'**: Used for emphasis or shock.
- **'Duhawm'**: Used in new contexts to mean something 'cool' or 'premium'.
- **'Chhiat loh'**: Used to describe someone who is attractive or well-dressed.
