# Zawlbuk as a Linguistic Metaphor

**Tags:** #linguistics #history

Many Mizo idioms carry the spatial logic of the **[[Zawlbuk]]**. To be "on the far side of the Zawlbuk" (*Zawlbuk chhak lam*) implies prominence or achievement, while "near the water pots" (*Tui bel bul*) implies low status or lack of courage.
