﻿# Linguistic Node: Ralte Dialect Khelte



## 1. Taxonomic Classification
- **Primary Group**: [[RalteLanguage]]
- **Regional Focus**: Northern Ridges
- **Common Status**: Northern Ralte

## 2. Phonetic Features
- **Key Marker**: Harsh 'kh' sounds, fast paced
- **Distinction**: Differs from **[[Lusei_Dialect_Aizawl|Standard Mizo]]** primarily in its tonal frequency and vowel elongation.

## 3. Sociolinguistic Context
Ralte Dialect Khelte is a critical component of the **[[Zohnahthlak]]** identity. It preserves archaic words lost in the standardized **[[Mizo_Dictionary_The_Final_Word|Duhlian]]** dialect.

## 4. Research Data (Tone Mapping)
As per the **[[Mizo_Computational_Linguistics_Dataset]]**, this dialect exhibits a higher frequency of the "Rising Tone" (*Lawn*) in its interrogative structures.
