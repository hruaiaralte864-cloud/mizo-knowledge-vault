# Neihdawn Village

**Tags:** #geography #village

**Neihdawn** is a scenic village in the Champhai district, known for its extensive terrace farming.

## Agriculture
While much of Mizoram still practices [[Jhum_Cultivation]], Neihdawn has been a leader in adopting permanent terrace farming for rice and vegetables, contributing significantly to the regional economy.
