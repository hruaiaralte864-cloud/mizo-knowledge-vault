# Demagiri (Tlabung) History



**Demagiri** (the Bengali name for **[[Tlabung_Town|Tlabung]]**) was the first site of a British 'frontier post' in the Lushai Hills. It served as the staging ground for the 1871 and 1891 expeditions that changed Mizo history forever.
