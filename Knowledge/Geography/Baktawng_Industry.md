# Baktawng (Chhuanthar Industry)

**Tags:** #economy #society

The **Chana Pawl** in Baktawng are known for their cottage industries: carpentry, furniture making, and steel fabrication. Their products are sold across the state and are noted for their exceptional craftsmanship.
