# Vaseitlang Village

**Tags:** #geography #village #southern_mizoram

**Vaseitlang** is a village situated in the southern part of Mizoram, within the Lawngtlai district. It is uniquely positioned in a region where the Mizo and Lai cultures intersect.

## Cultural Heritage
The village is known for its vibrant oral traditions and folk dances. It is a key location for observing the **[[Chheih_Lam]]** dance in its more traditional form. The elders of Vaseitlang are considered repositories of **[[Mizo_Oral_Tradition]]**, particularly stories relating to ancestral spirits and the jungle.

## Agriculture
Given its southern location, Vaseitlang benefits from a more tropical climate, allowing for the cultivation of rubber and betel nut, which are increasingly replacing traditional **[[Jhum_Cultivation]]**.

## Social Structure
The village adheres strictly to the **[[Tlawmngaihna]]** code, and the local YMA branch is among the most active in the district, organizing community service and forest conservation initiatives.
