# Tlawng River



The **Tlawng River** is the longest river within the borders of Mizoram. It flows from south to north, passing close to Aizawl.

## Utility
It is the primary source of water for the capital city. Its valleys are fertile, supporting the largest rice-growing areas in central Mizoram.
