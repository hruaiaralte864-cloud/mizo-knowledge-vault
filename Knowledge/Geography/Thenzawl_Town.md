# Thenzawl Town

**Tags:** #geography #textile #tourism

**Thenzawl** is one of the few large flat valleys in Mizoram and is a major center for **[[Puan|Mizo Handloom]]**.

## Handloom Hub
Most of the traditional Puans sold in Aizawl are woven in the thousands of household looms in Thenzawl. The town is Also home to the **Thenzawl Golf Resort** and is near the **[[Vantawng_Falls]]**.
