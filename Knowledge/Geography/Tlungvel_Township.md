# Tlungvel Township

**Tags:** #geography #village

**Tlungvel** is a major settlement in eastern Aizawl district. It is known for its high elevation and its role as a regional marketplace. The town has a strong tradition of community forest management, which has preserved much of the surrounding green cover.
