# Aizawl — Capital of Mizoram



**Aizawl** is the capital city and largest urban centre of Mizoram, home to approximately one-third of the entire state's population (~400,000 in the urban area).

## Physical Setting
The city is extraordinarily distinctive — built along a narrow, precipitous north-south ridge at elevations between 800m and 1,150m, overlooking:
- The **Tlawng River** valley to the west.
- The **Tuirial River** valley to the east.

Buildings cascade down near-vertical cliff faces, requiring an intricate network of stairs. Large-bore roads are almost impossible; many neighbourhoods are connected only by stone stairways.

## Founding
The British Army under Colonel C.G.A. Dixon established a garrison fort here in **1890** as a base for the second Lushai expedition.

## Key Institutions
- Mizoram University (National university)
- Regional Institute of Paramedical and Nursing Sciences (RIPANS)
- Synod offices of the Presbyterian Church
- Aizawl FC (football club, I-League champions 2016-17)
