# Hmuifang Peak (Tlang)

**Tags:** #geography #nature #tourism

**Hmuifang Peak** is one of the most popular hills in Mizoram, located about 50km from Aizawl.

## Recreation
The peak is covered in dense virgin forests and grassy meadows. It is managed as a tourist resort by the government and is a favorite for camping and mountain biking.
