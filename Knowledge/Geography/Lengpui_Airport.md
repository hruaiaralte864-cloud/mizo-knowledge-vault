# Lengpui Airport

**Tags:** #geography #transport #modern

**Lengpui Airport** is one of the most uniquely engineered airports in India. It is a "table-top" airport built into a flattened ridge near Aizawl.

## Features
- It is the first large airport in India built by a State Government.
- The runway is surrounded by deep gorges on both sides, making it a challenging but beautiful landing for pilots.
- It serves as the primary air link connecting Mizoram to Kolkata, Guwahati, and Delhi.
