# Mamit District

**Tags:** #geography #district

**Mamit** is a district in northwestern Mizoram bordering both Assam to the north and Bangladesh to the west. It is one of the least densely populated districts in the state, dominated by dense forest and the [[Dampa_Tiger_Reserve]].

## Kaladan Project
A section of the **Kaladan Multi-Modal Transit Transport Project** — India's ambitious infrastructure push to connect Mizoram to Southeast Asian markets via Myanmar's Sittwe port — traverses the district.

## Demographics
The district has a significant population of non-Mizo communities — Chakma, Hmar, and Biate communities — making it one of Mizoram's more ethnically diverse districts.
