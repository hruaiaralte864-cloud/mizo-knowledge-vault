# Pukzing Cave

**Tags:** #geography #nature #folklore

**Pukzing** is the largest and most famous cave in Mizoram, located in the Mamit district.

## The Legend of Mura
According to legend, the cave was the dwelling place of a giant, man-eating bird called **Mura**. The cave is so large that it is said a whole village could hide inside. It remains a major site of investigation for local historians and spelunkers.
