# Mara District River Systems

**Tags:** #geography #nature

The rivers of the Siaha district, primarily the **[[Chhimtuipui_River]]** and its tributaries, are characterized by deep rocky gorges and a staggered flow, making them ideal for future small-scale hydropower.
