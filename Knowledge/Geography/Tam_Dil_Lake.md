# Tam Dil Lake

**Tags:** #geography #nature #tourism

**Tam Dil** (literally "Mustard Lake") is a natural lake located near **Saitual** in Mizoram, approximately 85 km from [[Aizawl_District|Aizawl]]. It is named for the wild mustard plants (*Tam*) that grow abundantly along its shores.

## Features
- A serene, oval-shaped lake at 1,390 m elevation, surrounded by pine and oak forest.
- The lake supports boating, fishing, and is a major ecotourism and resort destination.
- Local legend holds that the lake was formed by a divine being who struck the earth and water burst forth.

It is consistently ranked among Mizoram's top three tourist destinations alongside the [[Phawngpui_Blue_Mountain|Blue Mountain]] and [[Vantawng_Falls]].
