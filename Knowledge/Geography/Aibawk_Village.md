# Aibawk Village

**Tags:** #geography #village

**Aibawk** is a scenic village in the Aizawl district, located on the way to the **[[Hmuifang_Mountain|Hmuifang Tlang]]**. It is a popular stop for tourists and is known for its traditional blacksmithing traditions.
