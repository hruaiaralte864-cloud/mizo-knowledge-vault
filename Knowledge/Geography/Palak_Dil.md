# Palak Dil (Palak Lake)



**Palak Dil** is the largest natural lake in Mizoram, situated in the Siaha district.

## Environment
The lake is surrounded by thick tropical evergreen forests and is a haven for migratory birds and rare flora. According to **[[Palak_Dil_Myth|Mara folklore]]**, the lake was created by a massive subsidence of land that swallowed a legendary village.

## Conservation
Currently, efforts are underway to declare the surrounding area a protected wetland to preserve its unique biodiversity.
