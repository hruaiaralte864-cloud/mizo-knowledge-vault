# Vantawng Khawhthla (Vantawng Falls)



**Vantawng Falls** is the highest and most spectacular waterfall in Mizoram, dropping over **220 meters**.

## Legend
Legend says it is named after a man named **Vantawnga**, who was famously skilled at jumping across the waterfall until he met a tragic end. The falls are surrounded by thick bamboo forests and are accessible from Thenzawl.
