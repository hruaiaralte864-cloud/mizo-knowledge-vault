# Khawlailung (Seat of Chiefs)

**Tags:** #history #geography

In the pre-colonial era, **Khawlailung** was a major fortified village belonging to the Sailo chiefs. Its ridge-top position was selected for its defensibility during the years of inter-tribal warfare.
