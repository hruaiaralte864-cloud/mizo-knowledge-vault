# Farkawn Village



**Farkawn** is a "village of legends" located in the Champhai district.

## Cultural Heritage
Farkawn and its surroundings are home to numerous historical sites:
- **Fiara Tui**: A legendary spring known for its clear water.
- **Kungawrhi Puk**: A cave associated with the folklore of [[Kungawrhi]].

The village is a major destination for cultural tourism in Mizoram.
