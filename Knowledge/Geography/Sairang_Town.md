# Sairang Town

**Tags:** #geography #transport #history

**Sairang** is a vital transit town located on the banks of the **[[Tlawng_River]]**, about 20km from Aizawl.

## Historical Hub
Historically, Sairang was the primary terminal for river navigation. Supplies from the plains of Assam were brought up the Tlawng River to Sairang and then carried by coolies or pack animals to the hill capital.

## Modern Development
Today, Sairang is a major logistics hub and the terminus for the new broad-gauge railway line being extended into Mizoram. It is the industrial gateway to the Aizawl district.
