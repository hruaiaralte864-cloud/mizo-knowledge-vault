# The Lote: Architecture of the Farm Hut



The **Lote** is a seasonal farm hut located deep in the cultivation fields, serving as a secondary home for Mizo farmers during the intense *Jhum* season.

## Architecture and Survival
Built primarily from **[[Mizo_Bamboo_Species_and_Use_Data]]**, the *Lote* is raised on stilts to prevent snake entry and provide a lookout over the crops. Its design priorities are high-airflow and thermal efficiency in the jungle heat.

## The Social Hub
The *Lote* is where the "Secret Folklore" of the jungle is passed down. It is a space for the **[[Mizo_Philosophy_of_Living]]** (hospitality) to be practiced in its purest form, where a passing traveler is always offered a meal of **[[Mizo_Cuisine_The_Logic_of_Bai]]**.
