# Khawzawl Town



**Khawzawl** is the district headquarters of the Khawzawl district. It has rapidly transformed from a village into a major urban center in eastern Mizoram.

## Economy
The town is a hub for the trade of ginger, chili, and handicrafts. It also hosts several major educational institutions that serve the eastern hill tracts.
