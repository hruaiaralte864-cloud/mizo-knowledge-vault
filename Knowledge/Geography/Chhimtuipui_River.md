# Chhimtuipui (Kaladan River)

**Tags:** #geography #nature #border

The **Chhimtuipui** is the largest river in Mizoram. In English, it is known as the **Kaladan**.

## Course
The river originates in the Chin State of Myanmar, flows through the Siaha and Lawngtlai districts of Mizoram, and then flows back into Myanmar to drain into the Bay of Bengal at Sittwe.

## Importance
It is the only river in Mizoram that is navigable for large portions, making it historically vital for trade. It is currently being developed as a major international transit route connecting Mizoram to the sea.
