# Khawhai Village

**Tags:** #geography #village

**Khawhai** is a major village in the Khawzawl district (formerly Champhai). It is strategically located on the main road connecting the central hills to the eastern borders.

## Topography
The village is perched on a high ridge, offering panoramic views of the surrounding hills. It is known for its cool climate and its vibrant youth community, which is very active in the **[[Young_Mizo_Association]]**.
