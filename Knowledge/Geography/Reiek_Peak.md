# Reiek Peak (Tlang)

**Tags:** #geography #nature #tourism

**Reiek Peak** is a spectacular ridge providing a bird's-eye view of Aizawl and the surrounding valleys.

## Cultural Value
At the foot of the peak lies a recreated **[[Traditional_Mizo_House|Traditional Mizo Village]]**, used for cultural education and as the site for the [[Antharium_Festival]].
