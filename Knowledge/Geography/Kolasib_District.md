# Kolasib District

**Tags:** #geography #district

**Kolasib** is a district in northern Mizoram, bordering Assam to the north.

## Strategic Position
Kolasib sits at the junction between the heavily tribal hills of Mizoram and the Barak Valley plains of Assam. The **Kaladan Multi-Modal Transit Transport Project** corridor passes through the district, connecting Mizoram to the deep-water port at Sittwe in Myanmar's Rakhine State — an infrastructure project that could transform the state's economic connectivity.

## Notable Person
**[[Jeremy_Lalrinnunga]]**, India's 2018 Youth Olympic Games gold medallist in weightlifting, was born in **Aizawl** but has deep family roots in the Kolasib belt.
