# Vaphai Village



**Vaphai** is a historical village located in the Champhai district, very close to the Myanmar border.

## Historical Significance
Vaphai was the site of the **Lianchhiari Lunglen Tlang**, a legendary peak where the heroine [[Lianchhiari_Legend|Lianchhiari]] was said to have watched her lover leave. The village takes great pride in its connection to Mizo folklore and maintains several memorial sites dedicated to these legends.
