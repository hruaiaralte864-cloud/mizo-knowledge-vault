# Baktawng Village

**Tags:** #geography #society

**Baktawng** is world-famous as the home of the **Chana Pawl** (the Chana Sect).

## The Largest Family
The village was home to the late **Pu Ziona**, who headed what was widely considered the world's largest family (hundreds of members living in a single massive house called *Chhuanthar Run*). The sect is known for its incredible work ethic, self-sufficiency, and discipline.
