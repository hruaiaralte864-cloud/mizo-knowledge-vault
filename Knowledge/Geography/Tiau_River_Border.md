# Tiau River (The International Border)



The **Tiau River** forms the international boundary between India (Mizoram) and Myanmar (Chin State).

## Economic Significance
Most cross-border trade occurs at the Tiau River bridge near **[[Champhai_District|Zokhawthar]]**. It is a lifeline for consumer goods and essential supplies.

## Cultural Link
Because people on both sides of the Tiau share the same ethnicity, language, and culture, the river is often described as "a bridge, not a barrier."
