# Siaha District

**Tags:** #geography #mara #governance

**Siaha** (formerly Saiha) is the southernmost district of Mizoram and the headquarters of the **Mara Autonomous District Council**.

## Unique Identity
Siaha is the heartland of the **Mara people**, who have a distinct language and culture within the Mizo family. The terrain is rugged, characterized by the deep valleys of the **[[Chhimtuipui_River|Chhimtuipui (Kaladan)]]** river.

## Notable Features
- **[[Palak_Dil]]**: The largest natural lake in the state is located here.
- **Cross-Border Trade**: Siaha is a major point for trade with the Chin and Rakhine States of Myanmar.
