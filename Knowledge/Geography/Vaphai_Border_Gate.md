# Vaphai Border Gate

**Tags:** #geography #border

The **Vaphai gate** is a traditional crossing point on the Tiau river. While less commercial than the Zokhawthar crossing, it remains vital for local inter-village trade and cultural exchange between the Mizo people of India and the Chin people of Myanmar.
