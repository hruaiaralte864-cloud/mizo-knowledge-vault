# Chawngte (Kamalanagar)

**Tags:** #geography #chakma

The town of **Chawngte** (officially Kamalanagar) is unique in Mizoram because it is divided by the Tuichawng river into two parts: one under the Lunglei district and the other under the Chakma ADC.
