# Biate Village



**Biate** (pronounced Bee-ah-tay) is famous throughout Mizoram and India as the **cleanest village in the state**, long before the national Swachh Bharat missions.

## The Character of Biate
The villagers of Biate have a decades-old self-imposed law regarding public hygiene. Every Saturday, the entire community gathers to clean the streets, drainage, and public spaces (see [[Inrinni_Culture]]).

## Economy
Biate is a major agricultural producer, known for its high-quality ginger and cabbage. It is located in the Champhai district and maintains a very high [[Mizoram_Literacy_and_Education|literacy rate]].
