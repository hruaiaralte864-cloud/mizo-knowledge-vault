# Lawngtlai District



**Lawngtlai** is located in southwestern Mizoram and serves as the headquarters of the **Lai Autonomous District Council**.

## Population
The district is predominantly inhabited by the **Lai people**. Like the Mara, they maintain a distinct identity and operate under the **[[Sixth_Schedule]]** of the Indian Constitution.

## Economy
The district's economy is heavily based on agriculture, particularly the growing of forest products and rubber. It is also the site of the **Kaladan Multi-Modal Transit Transport Project**, a massive infrastructure initiative connecting India to Southeast Asia.
