# Tlabung (Demagiri) Town



**Tlabung** is one of the most historically significant towns in western Mizoram.

## The British Gateway
During the 19th century, Tlabung was the primary entry point for British expeditions coming from Chittagong (now Bangladesh). It was a major river port on the **Karnaphuli** river.

## Modern Trade
Today, it remains a vital "border trade" town, linking Mizoram to the markets of the plains.
