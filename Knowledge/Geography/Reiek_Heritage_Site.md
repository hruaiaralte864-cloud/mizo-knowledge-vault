# Reiek — Heritage Village and Peak

**Tags:** #geography #culture #tourism

**Reiek** is a hill peak and heritage tourism village approximately **29 km from Aizawl**, offering some of the most dramatic panoramic views of the Mizo hills.

## The Heritage Village
At the summit is a reconstructed traditional Mizo village — a living-museum demonstrating:
- Traditional [[Traditional_Mizo_Architecture|Mizo house construction]] using bamboo and thatch.
- A recreated [[Zawlbuk]] (bachelors' dormitory).
- Demonstrations of [[Inbuan_Wrestling]] and [[Cheraw_Dance|Cheraw dance]].
- Traditional tools, granaries, and cooking methods.

## Trekking
The summit (1,548 m) is reached by a forest trail through oak and rhododendron forest. It is Mizoram's most accessible high-altitude hiking destination and offers views across to Bangladesh on clear winter days.
