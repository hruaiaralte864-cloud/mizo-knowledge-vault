# Serchhip District



**Serchhip** is a small but culturally significant district in central Mizoram, notable for having the **highest literacy rate** among all districts in the state — and by extension, one of the highest in India at over **98%**.

## The Literacy Story
The district's exceptional literacy rate is no accident. The community invested deeply in education generation after generation, building on the [[Missionary_Arrival|missionary education tradition]]. Local pride in this achievement is immense — it is embedded in the district's identity.

## Natural Attraction
The spectacular **[[Vantawng_Falls]]** — the tallest waterfall in Northeast India — is located in Serchhip district near the town of Thenzawl.
