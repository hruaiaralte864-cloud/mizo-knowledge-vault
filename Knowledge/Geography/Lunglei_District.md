# Lunglei — Southern Capital



**Lunglei** is the second-largest town in Mizoram and serves as the administrative and commercial hub of the southern region.

## Name Origin
The name derives from *Lung* (rock/stone) and *Lei* (bridge/platform) — a reference to a natural stone bridge formation found along the Tlawng River near the original settlement site.

## History
It was the **headquarters of the South Lushai Hills District** under the British, who established their southern command base here. During the [[MNF_Uprising_1966]], Lunglei was one of the primary targets of Operation Jericho and experienced intense fighting.

## Demographics
Lunglei is one of Mizoram's most ethnically diverse districts, with communities of Mizo Lusei, **Chakma**, **Mara**, and **Lai** peoples all represented.
