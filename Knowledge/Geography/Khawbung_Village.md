# Khawbung Village

**Tags:** #geography #village #history

**Khawbung** is one of the oldest and most historically significant villages in eastern Mizoram, situated in the Champhai district.

## History
Khawbung was a major hub for the Lusei chiefs during their early consolidation of power. It served as a vital stop on the migration path from the east. Many clans, including the [[Lusei_Clan|Sailo]] family, have deep ancestral roots here.

## Culture
The village is known for its preservation of Mizo oral traditions. It is near the site of the legendary **[[Rih_Dil_Legend|Rih Dil]]** lake (though the lake itself is across the border), and Khawbung has traditionally been the gathering point for travelers heading to the spirit lake.
