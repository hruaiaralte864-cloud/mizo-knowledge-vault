# Tiau River Crossing

**Tags:** #geography #border

The Tiau river is crossable at various points through suspension bridges. These bridges are more than just infrastructure; they are the lifelines of the **[[Mizo_Diaspora|Zo people]]** who live on both sides of the international boundary.
