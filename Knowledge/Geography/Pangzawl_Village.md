# Pangzawl Village



**Pangzawl** is a large village in the Lunglei district, famously associated with the tragedy of the **[[Lianchhiari_Legend|Lianchhiari]]** and **Chawngfianga** love story.

## Cultural Landmark
The village hosts several memorial sites related to the romantic folklore of the Mizo people. It is a major center for the preservation of traditional dances and has a very active **[[Young_Mizo_Association]]** chapter.
