# Darngawn Village



**Darngawn** is a picturesque village located in the Aizawl district. It is widely recognized for its agricultural productivity and the organized nature of its village administration.

## Model Village Status
Darngawn is often cited as a "model village" in Mizoram due to its successful implementation of rural development schemes. The village has achieved near-total literacy and has a robust waste management system, aligning with the **[[Mizo_Academy_of_Letters_MAL]]**'s goals for social improvement.

## Horticulture
The village is a major producer of exotic fruits like dragon fruit and passion fruit. These products are supplied to the **[[Aizawl_Market_Analysis]]** and exported to neighboring states.

## Historical Note
During the **[[Mizo_Insurgency_Movement]]**, Darngawn was one of the villages that provided refuge to displaced families, earning it a reputation for hospitality and humanitarianism.
