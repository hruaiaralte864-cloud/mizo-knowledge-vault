# Bilkhawthlir (Horticulture Hub)

**Tags:** #economy #geography

**Bilkhawthlir** has become a model for successful horticulture in Mizoram, with specialized farmers producing massive quantities of lemons and bananas for the Aizawl and Silchar markets.
