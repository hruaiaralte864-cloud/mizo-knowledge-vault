# Milu Puk (Cave of Heads)

**Tags:** #geography #history

Located in the Champhai district, **Milu Puk** is an ancient cave that, when discovered, was found to contain hundreds of human skulls. It is believed to be a site where heads taken in pre-colonial wars were stored or ritualized.
