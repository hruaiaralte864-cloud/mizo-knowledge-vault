# Khawzawl District

**Tags:** #geography #modern

**Khawzawl** is one of the newest districts in Mizoram, carved out of Champhai and Aizawl districts. It is known for its fertile valleys and its strategic location on the highway connecting Aizawl to the Myanmar border.
