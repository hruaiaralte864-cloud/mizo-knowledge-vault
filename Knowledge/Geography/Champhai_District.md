# Champhai — The Rice Bowl



**Champhai** is a district in eastern Mizoram that sits along the Myanmar border. It is widely known as the **"Rice Bowl of Mizoram"** due to the extraordinarily fertile Champhai Valley, which supports large-scale terraced wet-rice cultivation — unusual for a state dominated by [[Jhum_Cultivation]].

## Zokhawthar Border Trade
The border village of **Zokhawthar** is a critical India-Myanmar trade crossing point. Electronic goods, consumer products, and Southeast Asian commodities flow through this crossing, making Champhai a key economic node for the entire state.

## Sacred Access
Champhai is the starting point for pilgrimages to **[[Rih_Dil_Legend|Rih Dil]]**, the sacred lake of the dead located just across the border in Myanmar.
