# Saitual Township



**Saitual** is the headquarters of the Saitual district. It is one of the most planned and well-laid-out towns in Mizoram.

## Significance
Located on the highway between Aizawl and Champhai, it is a critical transit point. The town is known for its cool, misty weather and its vibrant church community and choir traditions.
