# Rungduala (The Strongman)



**Rungduala** is a legendary figure in Mizo storytelling, celebrated for his superhuman physical strength and his simple, honest character.

## Stories
Many Mizo "tall tales" involve Rungduala performing impossible feats, such as carrying a whole buffalo over a mountain or wrestling giant pythons. He is the Mizo equivalent of a "folk hero" whose stories taught the community the value of physical preparedness and humility.
