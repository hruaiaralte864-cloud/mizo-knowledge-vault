# The Myth of Palak Dil

**Tags:** #folklore #nature

According to Mara oral tradition, the site of **[[Palak_Dil]]** was once a bustling village.

## The Serpent's Curse
The legend tells of a giant serpent that lived under the village. After an act of greed or disrespect by the villagers, the ground beneath them began to shake and sink. In a single night, the entire village was swallowed by the earth, and the hollow was filled with water, creating the lake. It is said that on quiet nights, the spirits of the ancient villagers can still be heard.
