# Chawngtinleri (Queen of the Animals)



**Chawngtinleri** is a powerful figure in Mizo mythology, known as the goddess or queen who protects all the animals of the forest.

## The Keeper of the Hunt
Mizo hunters historically believed that they could only be successful if Chawngtinleri approved of the hunt. She was said to keep a "tally" of all kills. If a hunter was too greedy or broke the rules of nature, she would curse them or lead them into traps. She represents the ancient Mizo ethos of **[[Ethnobotany|environmental balance]]**.
