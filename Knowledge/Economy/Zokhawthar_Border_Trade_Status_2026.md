# Zokhawthar Border Trade: 2026 Market Dynamics



The **Zokhawthar** border crossing in **[[Champhai_District]]** is the primary "Valve" of the Mizo economy, connecting India with the Myanmar frontier.

## 2026 Status and Hostilities
As of 2026, the trade post is heavily influenced by the ongoing civil unrest in Myanmar. The border has become a critical hub for "Refugee Logistics" and cross-border essential services. Formal trade has slowed, but the "Grey Market" (unregulated trade) has reached record volumes.

## Commodity Flow
- **Imports**: Teak, precious stones, and cheap electronics from Southeast Asia.
- **Exports**: Indian pharmaceuticals, textiles (**[[Puanchei]]**), and processed foods.
- **Fuel Logistics**: Because of terrain difficulties, fuel from Zokhawthar often serves the eastern villages more efficiently than fuel from the Silchar-Aizawl road.

## Act East Policy Link
The modernization of Zokhawthar into an "Integrated Check Post" (ICP) is a cornerstone of the **[[Act_East_Policy_Strategic_Vision]]**, aiming to turn this frontier into a formal land-port to the East.
