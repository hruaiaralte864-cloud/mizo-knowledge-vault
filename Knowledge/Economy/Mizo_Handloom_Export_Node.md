## 2. Economic Significance
The Mizo Handloom Export Node is a key driver in the **[[Mizoram_State_Governance_Model|2026 Economic Vision]]**. It aims to diversify the state's revenue from traditional **[[Jhum_Cultivation_Cycle|Jhum Agriculture]]** to high-value **Trade** and digital exports.

## 3. Innovative Impact
Scaling traditional Puan weaving into the global premium textile market. By leveraging the **[[Mizoram_University_MZU|Academic Infrastructure]]**, this entity is fostering a new generation of **[[Aizawl_City_Data|Aizawl-based]]** entrepreneurs.

## 4. Global Connectivity
