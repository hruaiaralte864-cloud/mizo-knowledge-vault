﻿# Economy Node: Zotlang Industrial Estate



## 1. Sectoral Classification
- **Category**: Industry
- **Primary Hub**: Lunglei
- **Current Status**: Production Center

## 2. Economic Significance
The Zotlang Industrial Estate is a key driver in the **[[Mizoram_State_Governance_Model|2026 Economic Vision]]**. It aims to diversify the state's revenue from traditional **[[Jhum_Cultivation_Cycle|Jhum Agriculture]]** to high-value **Industry** and digital exports.

## 3. Innovative Impact
The primary manufacturing zone in southern Mizoram. By leveraging the **[[Mizoram_University_MZU|Academic Infrastructure]]**, this entity is fostering a new generation of **[[Aizawl_City_Data|Aizawl-based]]** entrepreneurs.

## 4. Global Connectivity
Under the **[[Greater_Mizo_Infrastructure_2026]]** plan, this sector is being positioned as a regional gateway for the **[[Indo-Myanmar_Frontier|Act East Policy]]**, connecting the Zo people to Southeast Asian markets.
