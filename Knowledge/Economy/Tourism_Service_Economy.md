# Economy Node: Tourism Service Economy

**Tags:** #economy #tech #startups #innovation #Mizoram #research #Aizawl #2026

## 1. Sectoral Classification
- **Category**: Service
- **Primary Hub**: Statewide
- **Current Status**: Tourism Hub

## 2. Economic Significance
The Tourism Service Economy is a key driver in the **[[Mizoram_State_Governance_Model|2026 Economic Vision]]**. It aims to diversify the state's revenue from traditional **[[Jhum_Cultivation_Cycle|Jhum Agriculture]]** to high-value **Service** and digital exports.

## 3. Innovative Impact
Ecotourism, adventure sports, and homestay-based revenue models. By leveraging the **[[Mizoram_University_MZU|Academic Infrastructure]]**, this entity is fostering a new generation of **[[Aizawl_City_Data|Aizawl-based]]** entrepreneurs.

## 4. Global Connectivity
Under the **[[Greater_Mizo_Infrastructure_2026]]** plan, this sector is being positioned as a regional gateway for the **[[Indo-Myanmar_Frontier|Act East Policy]]**, connecting the Zo people to Southeast Asian markets.
