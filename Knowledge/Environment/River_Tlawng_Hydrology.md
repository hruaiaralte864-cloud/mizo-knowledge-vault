## 2. Biological/Environmental Profile
The River Tlawng Hydrology is a critical component of the **[[Mizoram_Subtropical_Pine_Forest_Informatics|Zoram Biodiversity Network]]**. Located primarily in the **Central Mizoram** zones, it represents the high-density ecological wealth of the **[[Indo-Myanmar_Frontier|Hills]]**.

## 3. Scientific Significance
The longest river in the state, central to Aizawl's water supply and forest ecology. Its survival is linked to the **[[Jhum_Cultivation_Cycle|Sustainable Land Management]]** practices and the protection of the **[[Dampa_Tiger_Reserve_Geology|Core Habitat Zones]]**.
