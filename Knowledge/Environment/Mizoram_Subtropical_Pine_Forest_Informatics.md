## 2. Biological/Environmental Profile
The Mizoram Subtropical Pine Forest Informatics is a critical component of the **[[Mizoram_Subtropical_Pine_Forest_Informatics|Zoram Biodiversity Network]]**. ## 3. Scientific Significance
A technical analysis of the Pinus kesiya zones in the eastern hills. Its survival is linked to the **[[Jhum_Cultivation_Cycle|Sustainable Land Management]]** practices and the protection of the **[[Dampa_Tiger_Reserve_Geology|Core Habitat Zones]]**.

## 4. Conservation Status
Under the **[[Greater_Mizo_Infrastructure_2026]]** environmental guidelines, this Ecology is now monitored via the **[[Mizoram_Forest_Digital_Twin]]**, ensuring its preservation for future generations of the Zo people.
