## 2. Biological/Environmental Profile
The Vairengte Riverine Systems is a critical component of the **[[Mizoram_Subtropical_Pine_Forest_Informatics|Zoram Biodiversity Network]]**. Located primarily in the **Northern Border** zones, it represents the high-density ecological wealth of the **[[Indo-Myanmar_Frontier|Hills]]**.

## 3. Scientific Significance
The slow-moving river systems that define the northern plains of the state. Its survival is linked to the **[[Jhum_Cultivation_Cycle|Sustainable Land Management]]** practices and the protection of the **[[Dampa_Tiger_Reserve_Geology|Core Habitat Zones]]**.

## 4. Conservation Status
Under the **[[Greater_Mizo_Infrastructure_2026]]** environmental guidelines, this Environment is now monitored via the **[[Mizoram_Forest_Digital_Twin]]**, ensuring its preservation for future generations of the Zo people.
