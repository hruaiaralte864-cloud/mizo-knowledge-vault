# Kut — The Three Traditional Festivals

**Tags:** #culture #festival

The Mizo agricultural calendar was punctuated by three major festivals (*Kut*), each marking a key moment in the [[Jhum_Cultivation]] cycle:

| Festival | Timing | Occasion |
|---|---|---|
| **[[Chapchar_Kut]]** | March | After jungle-cutting, before burning |
| **[[Mim_Kut]]** | August–September | After maize harvest; honours the dead |
| **[[Pawl_Kut]]** | December–January | End of harvest season; thanksgiving |

All three festivals involve communal feasting, traditional dances (particularly **[[Cheraw_Dance|Cheraw]]**), and consumption of *[[Zu]]*.
