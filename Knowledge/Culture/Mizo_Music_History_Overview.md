# History of Mizo Music

**Tags:** #culture #music #history

Mizo music has evolved from the rhythmic chanting of the pre-Christian era to the sophisticated four-part harmony singing of the modern church.

## Eras
- **Pre-Christian**: Characterized by the *Khuang* (drum) and pentatonic chants.
- **Missionary Era**: Introduction of Western solfa and hymnody.
- **Modern Era**: Rise of Mizo pop, rock, and contemporary Christian music.
