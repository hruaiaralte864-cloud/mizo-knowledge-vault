# Traditional Game: Sakei-leh-Sia

**Tags:** #culture #sports #games #fitness #Mizoram #research #PhysicalEducation

## 1. Game Classification
- **Category**: Strategy
- **Primary Equipment**: Grid-based
- **Primary Focus**: Sophisticated tactical logic game.

## 2. Rules and Dynamics
The Sakei-leh-Sia is a traditional Mizo activity that emphasizes **Strategy**. Unlike modern sports, it is often designed to test the individual's **[[Tlawmngaihna]]** and resilience in rugged terrains.

## 3. Training and Philosophy
The 'Tiger and Bull' board game of the Lushai hills. In the ancient **[[Zawlbuk_Youth_Dormitory|Zawlbuk]]** system, these games served as the primary physical education for young men, preparing them for the duties of a **[[Pasaltha]]**.

## 4. Modern Revival
Today, these sports are showcased during festivals like **[[Chapchar_Kut_Festival]]**, ensuring that the physical heritage of the Zo people survives in the 21st century.
