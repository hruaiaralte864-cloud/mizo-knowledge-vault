# Khuallam (Dance of the Guests)



**Khuallam** is a traditional Mizo dance performed during the entry of guests into a village or during inter-community festivals.

## Performance
Dancers wear the traditional **[[Puandum]]** Puan and perform slow, rhythmic movements to the beat of a [[Traditional_Instruments|Khuang]] (drum). It is a dance of welcome, dignity, and communal harmony.
