# Young Mizo Association (YMA)



The **Young Mizo Association (YMA)** is the most powerful non-governmental organisation in Mizoram and one of the largest voluntary youth organisations in Asia, with over 600,000 members across thousands of branches worldwide.

Founded in **1935** in Aizawl, it operates as the de-facto moral guardian and civil society backbone of Mizo society — the direct institutional successor to the [[Zawlbuk]].

## Functions
- Organises collective funerals, disaster relief, and community self-help drives.
- Enforces Sunday worship — no commerce occurs on Sundays in Mizoram.
- Leads anti-drug, anti-alcohol, and anti-gambling campaigns.
- Maintains community cleanliness drives.
- Awards the prestigious **[[Tlawmngaihna_Award]]**.

It often wields more effective power in daily Mizo life than the state government itself.
