﻿# Mythology Node: Puk Zing The Abyss Cave



## 1. Narrative Classification
- **Category**: Geographical Legend
- **Key Entity**: Puk Zing
- **Narrative Focus**: Ancestral Memory and Spiritual Worldview.

## 2. Mythic Context
The Puk Zing The Abyss Cave is a foundational narrative in **[[Mizo_Life_and_Tradition]]**. These stories were traditionally passed down through oral storytelling in the **[[Zawlbuk_Youth_Dormitory|Zawlbuk]]**, serving as a moral and spiritual compass for the community.

## 3. Cultural Symbology
Famous for being the site where the great hero Khuangchera demonstrated his courage. This myth highlights the Mizo relationship with the **[[Mizoram_Subtropical_Pine_Forest|Natural World]]** and the belief in a thinning veil between the living and the spirits of the **[[Mithikhuah_City_of_Dead|Underworld]]**.

## 4. Modern Interpretation
