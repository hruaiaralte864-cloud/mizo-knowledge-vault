# Mizo Marriage Traditions



A Mizo wedding today is simultaneously a deeply customary and a modern Christian affair.

## Pre-Wedding Ceremony
The day before the church wedding, the **[[Man_Bride_Price|Man]]** payment ceremony is conducted formally between the two families, mediated by elders (*Upa*) from both sides. This is treated with great legal gravity.

## Church Wedding
A minister conducts a full Protestant wedding service. The occasion is a massive community event — the entire village or neighbourhood is invited and fed by the groom's family in a grand feast (*Mo lawm*).

## Reception Feast (*Mo Lawm*)
Mizo weddings are famous for serving generous quantities of rice, pork, chicken, and beef to hundreds or thousands of guests. Turning guests away is considered a profound social shame.
