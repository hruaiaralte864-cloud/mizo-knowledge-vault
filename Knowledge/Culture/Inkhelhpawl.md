﻿# Traditional Game: Inkhelhpawl



## 1. Game Classification
- **Category**: Team Strategy
- **Primary Equipment**: Bamboo/Stone
- **Primary Focus**: Early training for forest flanking maneuvers.

## 2. Rules and Dynamics
The Inkhelhpawl is a traditional Mizo activity that emphasizes **Team Strategy**. Unlike modern sports, it is often designed to test the individual's **[[Tlawmngaihna]]** and resilience in rugged terrains.

## 3. Training and Philosophy
A complex tag game with territory capture. In the ancient **[[Zawlbuk_Youth_Dormitory|Zawlbuk]]** system, these games served as the primary physical education for young men, preparing them for the duties of a **[[Pasaltha]]**.

## 4. Modern Revival
Today, these sports are showcased during festivals like **[[Chapchar_Kut_Festival]]**, ensuring that the physical heritage of the Zo people survives in the 21st century.
