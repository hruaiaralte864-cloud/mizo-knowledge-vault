# History of Mizo Cinema



Mizo cinema has progressed from early VHS recordings to sophisticated digital filmmaking. 

## Key Milestones
- **Phunchawng**: One of the earliest significant Mizo films.
- **Khopui**: An exploration of urban challenges in Aizawl.
- **Zozam Troupes**: Traveling drama groups that laid the foundation for screen acting.
