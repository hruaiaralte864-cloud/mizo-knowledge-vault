# The Mizo Soul (Mizo Nihphung)



An overarching synthesis of what it means to be Mizo. This node combines the concepts of **[[Tlawmngaihna]]**, the bravery of the **[[Pasaltha_Khuangchera]]**, and the communal joy of the **[[Kut_Festivals_Overview|Kut]]**.

## Core Pillars
1. **Selflessness**: The practice of putting others first.
2. **Resilience**: The survival spirit in the unpredictable terrain of the hills.
3. **Communalism**: The belief that "we are only as strong as the weakest member of our village."
