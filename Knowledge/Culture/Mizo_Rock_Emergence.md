# The Emergence of Mizo Rock

**Tags:** #culture #music #modern

In the 1970s and 80s, Aizawl became a hub for Western-style rock music. Bands like **Vanlalsailova** and others blended Mizo lyrics with heavy guitar riffs, reflecting the youth's desire for a modern identity.
