# Sarlamkai



**Sarlamkai** is an ancient war dance, primarily originating among the **Mara** and **Lai** communities of southern Mizoram.

## Context
Traditionally, it was performed after a successful hunt or victory in battle. Dancers carry spears and shields, re-enacting the motions of combat. Today, it is a spectacular cultural performance during state festivals.
