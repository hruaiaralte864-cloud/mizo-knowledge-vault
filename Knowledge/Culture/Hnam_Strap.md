﻿# Arts & Crafts Node: Hnam Strap



## 1. Technical Classification
- **Category**: Accessory
- **Primary Material**: Cane/Bark
- **Key Visual Marker**: Woven bark/fiber

## 2. Design and Engineering
The Hnam Strap represents the height of Mizo indigenous engineering. Whether it is the **Accessory** weaving techniques or the structural integrity of the **[[Bamboo_Ecology|Bamboo]]** work, each piece is designed for the rugged life of the **[[Mizoram_Subtropical_Pine_Forest|Zoram Hills]]**.

## 3. Cultural Value
The forehead strap used to carry heavy mountain loads. It is a critical component of the **[[Mizo_Life_and_Tradition]]** and is often presented during **[[Marriage_Customs_and_Bride_Price|Khurual]]** ceremonies.

## 4. Preservation
Modern designers in **[[Aizawl_City_Data]]** are now transitioning these patterns into high-fashion markets, ensuring the survival of the **[[Accessory]]** legacy.
