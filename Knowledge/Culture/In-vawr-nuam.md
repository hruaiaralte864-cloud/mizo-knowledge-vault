# Traditional Game: In-vawr-nuam

**Tags:** #culture #sports #games #fitness #Mizoram #research #PhysicalEducation

## 1. Game Classification
- **Category**: Physical
- **Primary Equipment**: Woven hemp
- **Primary Focus**: Reflex and peripheral vision development.

## 2. Rules and Dynamics
The In-vawr-nuam is a traditional Mizo activity that emphasizes **Physical**. Unlike modern sports, it is often designed to test the individual's **[[Tlawmngaihna]]** and resilience in rugged terrains.

## 3. Training and Philosophy
A game of tossing and catching small weighted pouches. In the ancient **[[Zawlbuk_Youth_Dormitory|Zawlbuk]]** system, these games served as the primary physical education for young men, preparing them for the duties of a **[[Pasaltha]]**.

## 4. Modern Revival
Today, these sports are showcased during festivals like **[[Chapchar_Kut_Festival]]**, ensuring that the physical heritage of the Zo people survives in the 21st century.
