# Arts & Crafts Node: Em Carrier

**Tags:** #culture #arts #crafts #weaving #Puan #basketry #Mizoram #research

## 1. Technical Classification
- **Category**: Basket
- **Primary Material**: Bamboo
- **Key Visual Marker**: Closed-weave conical

## 2. Design and Engineering
The Em Carrier represents the height of Mizo indigenous engineering. Whether it is the **Basket** weaving techniques or the structural integrity of the **[[Bamboo_Ecology|Bamboo]]** work, each piece is designed for the rugged life of the **[[Mizoram_Subtropical_Pine_Forest|Zoram Hills]]**.

## 3. Cultural Value
Main harvesting basket for the Jhum fields. It is a critical component of the **[[Mizo_Life_and_Tradition]]** and is often presented during **[[Marriage_Customs_and_Bride_Price|Khurual]]** ceremonies.

## 4. Preservation
Modern designers in **[[Aizawl_City_Data]]** are now transitioning these patterns into high-fashion markets, ensuring the survival of the **[[Basket]]** legacy.
