﻿# Traditional Game: Khuihlu Inkhelh



## 1. Game Classification
- **Category**: Skill/Targeting
- **Primary Equipment**: Local seeds
- **Primary Focus**: Precision and hand-eye coordination.

## 2. Rules and Dynamics
The Khuihlu Inkhelh is a traditional Mizo activity that emphasizes **Skill/Targeting**. Unlike modern sports, it is often designed to test the individual's **[[Tlawmngaihna]]** and resilience in rugged terrains.

## 3. Training and Philosophy
A tactical marble-like game using large forest seeds. In the ancient **[[Zawlbuk_Youth_Dormitory|Zawlbuk]]** system, these games served as the primary physical education for young men, preparing them for the duties of a **[[Pasaltha]]**.

## 4. Modern Revival
Today, these sports are showcased during festivals like **[[Chapchar_Kut_Festival]]**, ensuring that the physical heritage of the Zo people survives in the 21st century.
