# Inrinni (Saturday) Culture



In Mizoram, **Inrinni** (Saturday) has a special cultural significance stemming from the preparation for Sunday services.

## The Tradition
Saturday is the day for communal cleaning (*Hlo-vawh*), preparing firewood, and "clearing the path" for the Sabbath. In modern towns, this translates to a massive cleaning of houses and streets. It reflects the Mizo commitment to cleanliness and order.
