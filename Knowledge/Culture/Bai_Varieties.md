# Mizo Bai (The Soul Food)

**Tags:** #culture #food

**Bai** is the most common Mizo dish — a stew made from boiled vegetables, salt, and often flavored with chili and fermented pork.

## Varieties
- **Maian Bai:** Made from pumpkin leaves.
- **Antam Bai:** Made from mustard leaves and typically very bitter.
- **Siaha Bai:** A style from the south often incorporating dried fish.
