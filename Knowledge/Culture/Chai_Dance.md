# Chai Dance

**Tags:** #culture #dance

**Chai** is a community dance performed during the **[[Chapchar_Kut]]** festival. Dancers stand in a wide circle, linking arms or holding shoulders, and sway to the beat of gongs and drums while singing traditional songs. It is the ultimate expression of communal solidarity.
