## 1. Narrative Classification
- **Category**: Curse/Myth

## 2. Mythic Context
The Khawhring The Evil Eye is a foundational narrative in **[[Mizo_Life_and_Tradition]]**. These stories were traditionally passed down through oral storytelling in the **[[Zawlbuk_Youth_Dormitory|Zawlbuk]]**, serving as a moral and spiritual compass for the community.

## 3. Cultural Symbology
The belief in inherited evil spirits that can harm others via the gaze. This myth highlights the Mizo relationship with the **[[Mizoram_Subtropical_Pine_Forest|Natural World]]** and the belief in a thinning veil between the living and the spirits of the **[[Mithikhuah_City_of_Dead|Underworld]]**.

## 4. Modern Interpretation
Modern Mizo writers and artists in **[[Aizawl_City_Data]]** are now re-interpreting these legends in literature and animation, ensuring that the **[[Curse/Myth]]** legacy informs the digital future of the Zo people.
