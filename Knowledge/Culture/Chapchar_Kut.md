# Chapchar Kut — The Spring Festival



**Chapchar Kut** is the grandest and most joyful of the three traditional Mizo festivals (*Kut*). It is celebrated in **early March**, after the jungle has been felled for [[Jhum_Cultivation]] but before the brush is set on fire — during the brief waiting period known as *Chapchar* (when the cut branches dry in the sun).

## Activities
- Village-wide feasting and communal singing.
- Performance of the iconic **[[Cheraw_Dance]]** (Bamboo Dance).
- Mizo love song contests (*Hlado*).
- Drinking of traditional *[[Zu]]* (rice beer).

## Modern Celebration
Today Chapchar Kut is celebrated as a massive state festival in Aizawl stadium, attracting thousands of tourists. Traditional costumes (*[[Puan]]*), music, and dances fill the event.
