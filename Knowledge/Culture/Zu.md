# Zu — Traditional Rice Beer

**Tags:** #culture #food #tradition

**Zu** is the traditional fermented rice or maize beer of the Mizo people. Brewed domestically for generations, it was consumed during all [[Kut_Festivals_Overview|Kut festivals]], at the [[Zawlbuk]], and offered to guests as the highest expression of hospitality.

In the animist tradition, Zu was also offered as a libation to the spirits (*[[Ramhuai]]*) during rituals performed by the **Bawlpu** (shaman).

With the widespread adoption of [[Christianity_in_Mizoram|Christianity]], the Church actively discouraged and then prohibited the consumption of alcohol. Mizoram passed **prohibition laws** across several periods, lobbied by the [[Young_Mizo_Association]] and the Church. Today the state officially discourages alcohol, though consumption continues privately.
