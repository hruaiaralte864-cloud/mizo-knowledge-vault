# Chawlhni (Sunday/Sabbath) in Mizoram

**Tags:** #culture #religion #society

**Chawlhni** (Sunday) in Mizoram is a day of absolute quiet and sanctuary.

## The Atmosphere
Business stops. Public transport ceases. The streets of Aizawl, usually bustling, become silent. The day is entirely dedicated to church services and family. For many Mizos, this rhythmic "pause" in life is essential for mental and spiritual well-being.
