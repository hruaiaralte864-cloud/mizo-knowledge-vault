# Traditional Game: Sazu-leh-Sazupui

**Tags:** #culture #sports #games #fitness #Mizoram #research #PhysicalEducation

## 1. Game Classification
- **Category**: Social Game
- **Primary Equipment**: Group circle
- **Primary Focus**: Used for community bonding and forest instinct training.

## 2. Rules and Dynamics
The Sazu-leh-Sazupui is a traditional Mizo activity that emphasizes **Social Game**. Unlike modern sports, it is often designed to test the individual's **[[Tlawmngaihna]]** and resilience in rugged terrains.

## 3. Training and Philosophy
A predator-prey roleplay game. In the ancient **[[Zawlbuk_Youth_Dormitory|Zawlbuk]]** system, these games served as the primary physical education for young men, preparing them for the duties of a **[[Pasaltha]]**.

## 4. Modern Revival
Today, these sports are showcased during festivals like **[[Chapchar_Kut_Festival]]**, ensuring that the physical heritage of the Zo people survives in the 21st century.
