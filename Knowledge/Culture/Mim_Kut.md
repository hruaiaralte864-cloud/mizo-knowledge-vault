# Mim Kut



**Mim Kut** is a festival dedicated to the remembrance of ancestors and deceased loved ones.

## Traditions
It is traditionally celebrated in August or September. Offerings of food and drink were made to the spirits of the dead. Today, while the specific animist rituals have faded, the sentiment of honoring ancestry remains a core part of the Mizo identity.
