# Pawl Kut



**Pawl Kut** is the harvest festival of the Mizo people, traditionally celebrated in December after the grain is safely stored.

## The Festival of Children
Historically, Pawl Kut was a festival where children were given special attention and feasts. It was a time of thanksgiving for the abundance of the year and for the health of the community's future generation.
