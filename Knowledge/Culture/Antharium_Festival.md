# Anthurium Festival



The **Anthurium Festival** is a modern state festival celebrated at the **Reiek Hills** near Aizawl.

## Purpose
While traditional festivals like [[Chapchar_Kut]] are ancient, the Anthurium Festival was created to promote tourism and the state's thriving floriculture industry. It showcases traditional dances, music, and the vast variety of Anthuriums grown in the state.
