# Kawrchei



**Kawrchei** is the traditional blouse worn by Mizo women, specifically designed to match the **[[Puanchei]]**.

## Design
It is a hand-woven masterpiece featuring colorful horizontal patterns and embroidery. In modern Mizoram, the Kawrchei is widely used for formal occasions and is an essential part of the Mizo female identity.
