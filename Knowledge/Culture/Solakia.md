# Solakia



**Solakia** is another powerful war dance closely related to [[Sarlamkai]]. It is most famously practiced by the Mara people in the Siaha district.

## Ritual Significance
The dance was historically tied to the capture of an enemy's head, celebrating the bravery of the warrior. It involves intense rhythmic stepping and chanting.
