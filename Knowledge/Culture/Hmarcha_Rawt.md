# Hmarcha Rawt

**Tags:** #culture #food

**Hmarcha Rawt** is the essential Mizo chili chutney. No Mizo meal is truly complete without it.

## Preparation
It is made by roasting or boiling local green or red chilies and crushing them with salt, ginger, and often **Sa-um** (fermented pork fat). It is known for its intense heat and smoky flavour.
