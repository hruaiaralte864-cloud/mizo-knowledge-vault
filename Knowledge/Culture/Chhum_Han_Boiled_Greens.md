# Culinary Node: Chhum Han Boiled Greens

**Tags:** #culture #cuisine #culinary #food #Mizoram #research #Nutrition

## 1. Gastronomic Profile
- **Category**: Side Dish
- **Key Ingredients**: Water, Local greens
- **Flavor Profile**: Earthy, Pungent (Sa-um based), and highly Nutritious.

## 2. Preparation Science
The Chhum Han Boiled Greens represents the traditional Mizo philosophy of "Oil-Free" cooking. By utilizing **[[Sa-um_Fermentation|Sa-um]]** or **[[Bekang_Fermented_Soya|Bekang]]** as a natural flavor enhancer, the Mizo developed a high-density, low-fat diet optimized for mountain labor.

## 3. Cultural Context
Plain boiled vegetables, preserved the natural flavor of the hills. It is a critical component of the **[[Mizo_Life_and_Tradition]]** and is central to the communal dining experience (*Puipun*).

## 4. Health and Biodiversity
Most ingredients are sourced directly from the **[[Jhum_Cultivation_Cycle]]** or the **[[Hmuifang_Forest_Reserve]]**, ensuring a diet rich in organic phytonutrients.
