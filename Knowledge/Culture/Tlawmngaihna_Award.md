# The Tlawmngaihna Award



The **Tlawmngaihna Award** is the highest civil honour in Mizo society, awarded by the **[[Young_Mizo_Association]]** to individuals who perform extraordinary acts of selfless heroism aligned with the ideals of **[[Tlawmngaihna]]**.

## Criteria
Recipients are judged to have demonstrated the highest levels of:
- Selfless sacrifice with no expectation of reward.
- Physical courage in saving others at personal risk.
- Sustained service to the community over time.

## Notable Recipients
Recipients have included:
- Individuals who jumped into swollen rivers to save drowning strangers.
- Nurses who worked without pay during disease outbreaks in remote villages.
- People who sheltered and fed [[Myanmar_Border_Relations|Chin refugees]] from Myanmar after the 2021 coup at personal cost.

The award carries no monetary component — it consists only of a citation and a publicly presented *[[Puan]]* — making its moral weight all the more powerful in Mizo estimation.
