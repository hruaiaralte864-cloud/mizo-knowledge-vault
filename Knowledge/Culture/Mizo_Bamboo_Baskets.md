# Mizo Bamboo Basketry



Traditional Mizo life was facilitated by a sophisticated variety of bamboo baskets, each with a specific purpose.

## Key Types
- **Thlangra:** A shallow, flat tray used for winnowing grain.
- **Fawng:** A conical basket carried with a headstrap, used for gathering firewood or shifting harvest.
- **Tlamen:** A large, sturdy basket used for storing grain in the granary.
