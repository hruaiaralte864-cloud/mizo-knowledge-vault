# Chheihlam Hla (Drunkard's Songs)



Despite the name, **Chheihlam Hla** are communal songs sung during the *Chheihlam* dance. They involve a lead singer improvising lyrics while the crowd joins in the chorus with clapping and rhythmic stomping.
