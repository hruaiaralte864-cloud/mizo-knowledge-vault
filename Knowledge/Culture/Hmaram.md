# Hmaram

**Tags:** #culture #textile #art

**Hmaram** is an ancient Mizo Puan design, primarily associated with the **[[Hmar_Clan]]**, but adopted widely across all Mizo groups.

## Features
It is traditionally a dark blue or black cloth with small, intricate white or red embroidery-like patterns along the edges. It is one of the oldest recorded patterns in Mizo oral history.
