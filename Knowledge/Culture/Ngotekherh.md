# Ngotekherh

**Tags:** #culture #textile #art

**Ngotekherh** is a classic Mizo Puan, easily recognized by its elegant black and white pattern.

## The Pattern
It features multiple thin black horizontal lines on a white background, with a distinct black border. Historically, the Ngotekherh was a common daily-wear Puan for both men and women, prized for its durability and simple elegance.

## Modern Use
Today, while no longer used as daily wear, it remains a staple for church services and formal gatherings. It is considered the most "stately" and formal of the traditional designs.
