# ISRO Regional Academic Centre for Space (RAC-S)

**Regional Academic Centre for Space (RAC-S)** at **[[NIT_Mizoram_Overview]]** is a strategic initiative by the **Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO)** to foster space-tech excellence in Northeast India.

## Mission and Research
The center acts as a hub for advanced research in satellite communication, remote sensing, and space-grade materials. Mizo researchers are collaborating on projects that optimize satellite telemetry for mountain terrains, ensuring better connectivity for the **[[Digital_Hill_State_Vision]]**.

## Impact on Education
The establishment of RAC-S has transformed the **[[NIT_Mizoram_Overview]]** into a premier technical destination. It offers undergraduate and postgraduate students the opportunity to participate in ISRO’s student satellite programs. This is a core pillar of the **[[Mizoram_Socio-Economic_Development_Policy_SEDP]]**'s goal to create "Future-Ready Mizo Technologists."

## Strategic Positioning
Mizoram's clear mountain air and high-altitude locations provide unique vantage points for ground-station telemetry research, directly supporting the **[[Mizoram_2050_Projections]]** for a knowledge-driven economy.
