# Civil Hospital, Aizawl

**Tags:** #healthcare #modern #society

The oldest and largest government hospital in Mizoram. In the Mizo community context, 'Civil Hospital' is often the center of massive voluntary efforts, with local YMA branches often providing meals and care for patients from rural districts.
