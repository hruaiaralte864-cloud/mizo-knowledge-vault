# Zoramthanga (born 1944)

**Tags:** #biography #politics

**Zoramthanga** is one of the most consequential political figures in contemporary Mizo history. He served as Chief Minister of Mizoram for two terms (1998–2003 and 2003–2008) and again from 2018 to 2023.

He was a close lieutenant of [[Laldenga]] during the underground insurgency, serving as a senior MNF commander in the jungles and later in diplomatic roles abroad.

## Legacy
As Chief Minister, he is credited with:
- Infrastructure development, including the construction of roads into remote areas.
- The **New Land Use Policy (NLUP)** — an ambitious scheme to transition shifting cultivators from [[Jhum_Cultivation]] to settled horticulture and cash-crop farming.
- Negotiating the **Bru (Reang) Refugee Settlement Agreement** (2020).
