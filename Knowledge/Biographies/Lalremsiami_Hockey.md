# Lalremsiami (The Hockey Star)



**Lalremsiami** (born 2000) is a professional hockey player and a key forward for the Indian Women's National Team. She is the first Mizo woman to compete in the Olympic Games.

## Recognition
- **FIH Rising Star of the Year (2019)**: The first Indian woman to win this award.
- **Padma Shri**: Awarded for her exceptional contributions to sports.
