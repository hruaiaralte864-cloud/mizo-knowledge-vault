# Rev. Chuautera (Spiritual Pioneer)

**Tags:** #biography #religion

**Rev. Chuautera** was one of the first Mizo converts and a tireless early missionary within his own people. He is credited with bringing the gospel to some of the remotest corners of the Lushai Hills, often traveling on foot for weeks at a time to reach uncontacted villages.
