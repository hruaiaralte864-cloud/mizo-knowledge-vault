# Suakpuilala (The Great Diplomat)

**Tags:** #biography #history #chief

**Suakpuilala** was a prominent Sailo chief in the late 19th century, known for his complex interactions with the British colonial administration.

## Interactions with the British
Unlike some chiefs who chose open warfare, Suakpuilala was a master of diplomacy. He was the first major chief to sign a formal treaty with the British in the 1870s, establishing a 'frontier' and facilitating trade.

## Impact
His decisions delayed the full military annexation of the Lushai Hills by decades, allowing the Mizo people more time to adapt to the changing political landscape of the Indian subcontinent.
