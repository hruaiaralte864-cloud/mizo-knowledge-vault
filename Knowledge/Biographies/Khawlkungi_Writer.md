# Khawlkungi (Literary Pioneer)

**Tags:** #biography #literature

**Khawlkungi** was one of the first Mizo women to achieve national recognition for her writing. Her short stories and essays often explored the changing role of women in Mizo society and the tensions between tradition and modernity. She is a recipient of the Padma Shri.
