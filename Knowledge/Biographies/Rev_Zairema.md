# Rev. Zairema



**Rev. Zairema** (1917–2008) was one of the greatest Mizo intellectuals and church leaders of the 20th century.

## Contributions
- **Translation**: He played a central role in the revision and refinement of the Mizo Bible.
- **Ecumenism**: He was a bridge-builder between different denominations.
- **Social Thought**: His writings on Mizo identity and the relationship between culture and Christianity remain foundational.

He was the first Mizo to hold a Doctorate in Divinity and served as a high-ranking official in the Presbyterian Church of India.
