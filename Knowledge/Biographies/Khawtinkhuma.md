# Khawtinkhuma (Union Leader)



**Khawtinkhuma** was a prominent leader of the Mizo Union during the critical years of Indian independence. He was a key negotiator in the process of Mizoram joining the Indian Union and was a vocal advocate for the rights of the hill tribes.
