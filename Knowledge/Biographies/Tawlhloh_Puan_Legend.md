# The Legend of the Tawlhloh Puan



While the **Tawlhloh Puan** is a textile, its name and origin are tied to the legendary warrior **Pu Pawla**.

## Meaning of the Name
'Tawlhloh' means "unyielding" or "not to be pushed back." Traditionally, this special woven cloth could only be worn by a warrior who had demonstrated extreme bravery in battle.

## The Warrior's Vow
A warrior wearing the Tawlhloh Puan was making a public vow: "I will not retreat. I will advance or I will die on this spot." It is the most prestigious of all traditional Mizo Puans, symbolising the ultimate commitment to the village and the clan.
