# Vankhama (The Romantic Poet)



**Vankhama** is remembered as the premier romantic poet and songwriter of the mid-20th century. His lyrics often used nature as a metaphor for love and longing. He was a master of the Mizo language, utilizing classical metaphors (*Tawng Upa*) that other writers struggled to master.
