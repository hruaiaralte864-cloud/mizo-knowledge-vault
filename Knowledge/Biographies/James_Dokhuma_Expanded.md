# James Dokhuma (The Voice of an Era)



**James Dokhuma** (1932–2007) is considered the most prolific and influential Mizo writer of the modern age.

## Works
He wrote over 30 books, including novels, plays, and histories. His historical novels, such as **'Tharkungi'**, are credited with keeping Mizo ancestral history alive for the younger generation. He was awarded the **Padma Shri** for his monumental contributions to Mizo literature.
