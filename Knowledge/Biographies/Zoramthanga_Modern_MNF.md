# Zoramthanga (MNF Leader & CM)

**Tags:** #biography #politics #modern

**Zoramthanga** was the right-hand man of [[Laldenga_Detail|Laldenga]] during the insurgency years. After the peace accord, he emerged as the leader of the MNF and served as the Chief Minister of Mizoram for multiple terms. He is known for his political cunning and his focus on regional development.
