# Laldenga (Father of Mizo Statehood)

**Tags:** #biography #politics #insurgency

**Laldenga** (1927–1990) is perhaps the most famous political figure in Mizo history.

## The MNF Years
He founded the **[[Mizo_National_Front]]** (MNF) and led the 20-year armed struggle for independence. He was a masterful orator and a strategist who managed to keep the Mizo cause on the international stage.

## The Peace Accord
In 1986, he signed the **Mizoram Peace Accord** with the Government of India, ending the insurgency and leading to the creation of Mizoram as a full state. He served as the first Chief Minister of the State of Mizoram.
