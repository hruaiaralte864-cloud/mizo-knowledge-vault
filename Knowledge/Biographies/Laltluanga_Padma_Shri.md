# Laltluanga (Padma Shri)

**Tags:** #biography #literature

**Laltluanga** was an early Mizo intellectual and social reformer who received the **Padma Shri** for his work in education and social welfare. He is remembered as one of the visionaries who helped transition Mizo society into the 20th century.
