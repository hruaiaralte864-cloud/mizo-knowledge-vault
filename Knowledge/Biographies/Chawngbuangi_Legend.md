# Chawngbuangi (The Heroine)

**Tags:** #biography #folklore

**Chawngbuangi** is a legendary heroine of Mizo folklore, celebrated for her beauty and her tragic love story. Like the stories of Liana and Thangi, her tale remains a core part of the Mizo romantic imagination.
