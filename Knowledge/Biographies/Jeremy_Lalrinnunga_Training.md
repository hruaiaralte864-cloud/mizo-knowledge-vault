# Jeremy Lalrinnunga: The Training of a Champion

**Tags:** #biography #sports #weightlifting

**Jeremy Lalrinnunga**, the Commonwealth Games gold medalist from Aizawl, has become a symbol of dedication and technical precision in Indian weightlifting.

## Early Regimen
Born into a family with a strong sporting background (his father was a national-level boxer), Jeremy began his training at a very young age in the local gymnasiums of Aizawl. His early training focused on flexibility and core strength, utilizing the natural hilly terrain of **[[Aizawl_District]]** for endurance.

## Technical Mastery
Under the mentorship of national coaches at the Army Sports Institute, Jeremy developed a unique "explosive" lifting style. His ability to maintain balance under extreme loads is often attributed to his high neural-muscular coordination, a trait highly valued in **[[Mizo_Sports_Culture]]**.

## Impact on Weightlifting
Jeremy's success at the Youth Olympics and the Commonwealth Games has led to a surge in weightlifting academies in Mizoram. The government has prioritized the development of **[[Synthetic_Track_Development_Sports]]** and indoor arenas following his international success.
