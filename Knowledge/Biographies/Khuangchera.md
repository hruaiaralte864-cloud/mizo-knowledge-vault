# Khuangchera — The Last Warrior Chief (c.1850–1890)

**Tags:** #biography #military #history

**Khuangchera** is the greatest war-hero in Mizo historical memory. A physically imposing and deeply brave leader from Ailawng village, he led the last organised Mizo military resistance against the British expeditionary columns during the [[Lushai_Expeditions]].

During the decisive **Battle of Changsil (1890)**, Khuangchera and his companion **Ngurbawnga** launched ferocious counter-attacks against the advancing British column despite overwhelming firepower. Both were killed in combat.

The British officers who fought against him recorded admiration for his extraordinary courage in their official dispatches.

Today he is celebrated as Mizoram's founding martyr, embodying [[Tlawmngaihna]] in its purest military form.
