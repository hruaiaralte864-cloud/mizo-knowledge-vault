# R. Vanlawma (Political Thinker)



**R. Vanlawma** was one of the founders of the **[[Mizo_Union]]**, the first political party in Mizoram. He was a visionary who advocated for Mizo self-determination and the abolition of the hereditary chief system. His writings laid the intellectual groundwork for the Mizo movement for statehood.
