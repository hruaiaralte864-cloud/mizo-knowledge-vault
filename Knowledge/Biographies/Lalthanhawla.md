# Lal Thanhawla (Five-Time Chief Minister)

**Tags:** #biography #politics #modern

**Lal Thanhawla** is the longest-serving Chief Minister of Mizoram.

## Impact
He is credited with the stabilization of the state post-1986 and the massive infrastructure development of the 1990s and 2000s. He was a key figure in the **Congress Party** in Northeast India and a strong advocate for sports and youth development.
