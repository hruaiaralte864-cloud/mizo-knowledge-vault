- **Name**: Chawngbawla
- **Era**: 18th Century
- **Title/Role**: Pasaltha

## 3. Cultural Impact
Chawngbawla remains a symbol of **[[Tlawmngaihna]]** and the resilient spirit of the Zo people. Their life is often taught as a pillar of **[[Mizo_Life_and_Tradition]]**.
