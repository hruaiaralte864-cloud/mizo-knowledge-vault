﻿# Biography: Aiburuma



## 1. Profile Summary
- **Name**: Aiburuma
- **Era**: 18th Century
- **Title/Role**: Chief / Strategist
- **Primary Region**: Western fringes

## 2. Historical Achievement
Navigated the early complex relations between the Lushai and outside kingdoms.

## 3. Cultural Impact
Aiburuma remains a symbol of **[[Tlawmngaihna]]** and the resilient spirit of the Zo people. Their life is often taught as a pillar of **[[Mizo_Life_and_Tradition]]**.

## 4. Legacy
Memorialized in contemporary Mizo society through songs, school curriculum, and the **[[Young_Mizo_Association]]** honors.
