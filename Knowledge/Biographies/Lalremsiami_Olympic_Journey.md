# Lalremsiami: The Olympic Hockey Pioneer



**Lalremsiami**, a striker for the Indian National Women's Hockey team, is the first Mizo woman to compete in the Olympics. Born in Kolasib, her journey from a remote village to the Tokyo 2020 Olympics is a story of immense grit and sacrifice.

## The Tokyo 2020 Historic Run
Lalremsiami was a key player in the Indian team that reached the semi-finals for the first time in Olympic history. Her speed and opportunistic scoring made her a global standout. She was named the "FIH Rising Star of the Year" in 2019, the first Indian woman to receive the honor.

## Overcoming Adversity
During the FIH Series Finals in 2019, her father passed away. In an act of true **[[Tlawmngaihna]]**, she chose to stay and play for the country, helping India qualify for the Olympics before returning home. This moment cemented her status as a cultural hero in Mizoram.

## Legacy
Her success has triggered a massive interest in field hockey across the state, leading to the establishment of specialized training centers in the **[[Kolasib_District]]**.
