﻿# Biography: Lalsangzuali Sailo



## 1. Profile Summary
- **Name**: Lalsangzuali Sailo
- **Era**: 20th Century
- **Title/Role**: Cultural Icon / Singer
- **Primary Region**: Aizawl

## 2. Historical Achievement
Padma Shri awardee who preserved Mizo folk songs on the national stage.

## 3. Cultural Impact
Lalsangzuali Sailo remains a symbol of **[[Tlawmngaihna]]** and the resilient spirit of the Zo people. Their life is often taught as a pillar of **[[Mizo_Life_and_Tradition]]**.

## 4. Legacy
Memorialized in contemporary Mizo society through songs, school curriculum, and the **[[Young_Mizo_Association]]** honors.
