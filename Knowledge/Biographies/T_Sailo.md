# Brigadier T. Sailo

**Tags:** #biography #politics #military

**Brigadier T. Sailo** (1922–2015) was a unique figure who served in the Indian Army before returning to Mizoram to enter politics.

## Contributions
He founded the **People's Conference** party and served as Chief Minister. He was a champion of human rights and was incredibly vocal against the excesses of the security forces during the insurgency years. He is also remembered for his "clean politics" mission.
