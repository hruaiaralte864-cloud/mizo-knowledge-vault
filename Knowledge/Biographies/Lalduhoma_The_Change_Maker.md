# Lalduhoma: The Change Maker



**Lalduhoma** is a transformative political leader in Mizoram, currently serving as the Chief Minister. A former IPS officer who once served in the Prime Minister's security detail, his career is defined by his commitment to radical administrative reform.

## The ZPM Revolution
Lalduhoma founded the **[[Zoram_People_s_Movement_ZPM]]**, which challenged the long-standing duopoly of the MNF and Congress. His campaign centered on the "New System" (*Kalphung Thar*) and the **"Handholding Scheme"** (see **[[Mizoram_Budget_2025_26_Analysis]]**), promising a government that stands with the common man.

## Anti-Defection Legacy
In 1988, Lalduhoma became the first Member of Parliament in India to be disqualified under the anti-defection law—a fact he often cites as a badge of honor for standing by his principles. His victory in the 2023 elections is seen as a turning point in **[[Mizoram_Political_History]]**, moving the state toward governance-based rather than sentiment-based voting.

## Governance Style
Known for his accessibility and no-nonsense approach to corruption, Lalduhoma’s administration is currently implementing the most significant fiscal restructuring in the state's history.
