# K.C. Lalvunga (The Diplomat-Writer)



**K.C. Lalvunga** was a member of the Indian Foreign Service who also achieved greatness as a Mizo writer. He was known for bringing a global perspective to Mizo literature and advocating for the international recognition of Mizo culture.
