## 1. Profile Summary
- **Name**: Chawngthuangpa
- **Era**: 19th Century
- **Title/Role**: Pasaltha
- **Primary Region**: Siaha region

## 2. Historical Achievement
Renowned for silent ambush tactics during the chieftainship wars.

## 3. Cultural Impact
Chawngthuangpa remains a symbol of **[[Tlawmngaihna]]** and the resilient spirit of the Zo people. Their life is often taught as a pillar of **[[Mizo_Life_and_Tradition]]**.

## 4. Legacy
Memorialized in contemporary Mizo society through songs, school curriculum, and the **[[Young_Mizo_Association]]** honors.
