# Rokunga (The Bard of Mizoram)

**Tags:** #biography #music #patriotism

**Rokunga** (1914–1969) is the most revered songwriter in Mizo history.

## Musical Legacy
His songs, which range from Christian hymns to patriotic anthems, are sung at every major Mizo gathering. His compositions like "Ro min rel sak ang che" are considered unofficial national anthems of the Mizo spirit. He was posthumously honored for his role in defining Mizo cultural identity through song.
