# Brigadier Thenphunga Sailo AVSM (1922–2015)



**Brigadier T. Sailo** was the first Mizo to be commissioned as an officer in the Indian Army, rising to the rank of Brigadier. He earned the **Ati Vishisht Seva Medal (AVSM)**.

## The Human Rights Crusader
During the [[MNF_Uprising_1966]], when the Indian Army carried out widespread human rights abuses during [[Village_Grouping]], Sailo set up the **Human Rights Committee** and personally documented atrocities. He presented a damning report directly to Prime Minister Indira Gandhi, leading to a significant reduction in military excesses.

## Chief Minister
He founded the **People's Conference (PC)** party and served as Chief Minister twice (1978–1979 and 1979–1984). He was known for his extraordinary personal discipline, incorruptibility, and administrative rigour.
