# Ps. Thatuma (Hymn Writer)

**Tags:** #biography #religion #music

**Ps. Thatuma** was a legendary figure in the early Mizo church, known for composing hymns that were uniquely Mizo in their melody and structure, moving away from purely Western translated tunes. His hymns are still the backbone of congregational singing in Mizoram.
