# Laltluangliana Khiangte (Scholar & Poet)



**Laltluangliana Khiangte** is a contemporary Mizo academic, playwright, and poet. He has been a champion of the Mizo language at the national level and served as the President of the Mizo Academy of Letters. He is known for his work in documenting and reviving traditional Mizo folk plays.
