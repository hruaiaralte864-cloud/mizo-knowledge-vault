# Biography: P S Thangkhuma

**Tags:** #history #biography #leader #Pasaltha #culture #Mizoram #research

## 1. Profile Summary
- **Name**: P S Thangkhuma
- **Era**: Late 20th Century
- **Title/Role**: Political Leader
- **Primary Region**: Aizawl

## 2. Historical Achievement
Key architect of the early political unification of the hills.

## 3. Cultural Impact
P S Thangkhuma remains a symbol of **[[Tlawmngaihna]]** and the resilient spirit of the Zo people. Their life is often taught as a pillar of **[[Mizo_Life_and_Tradition]]**.

## 4. Legacy
Memorialized in contemporary Mizo society through songs, school curriculum, and the **[[Young_Mizo_Association]]** honors.
