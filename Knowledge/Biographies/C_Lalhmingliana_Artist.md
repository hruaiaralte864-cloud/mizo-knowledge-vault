# C. Lalhmingliana (The Painter)



**C. Lalhmingliana** is one of the most celebrated contemporary painters in Mizoram. He is known for his stunning realistic and semi-abstract depictions of Mizo village life, traditional dances, and the misty hills. His work has been exhibited at the national level and he is a mentor to many young Mizo artists.
