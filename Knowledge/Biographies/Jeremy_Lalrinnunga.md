# Jeremy Lalrinnunga (Weightlifter)

**Tags:** #biography #sports

**Jeremy Lalrinnunga** (born 2002) is a weightlifter who won India's first-ever gold medal at the Youth Olympic Games in 2018. He also won gold at the 2022 Commonwealth Games. He is a symbol of the physical discipline and strength inherent in the Mizo sporting culture.
