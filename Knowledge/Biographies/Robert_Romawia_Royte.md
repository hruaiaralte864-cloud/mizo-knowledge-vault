# Robert Romawia Royte

**Tags:** #biography #politics #sports

A prominent politician and business tycoon who served as the Sports Minister of Mizoram. He is known for his large-scale investments in Mizo football and his role in the development of the **[[Aizawl_FC]]** club, which became the first Northeast team to win the I-League.
