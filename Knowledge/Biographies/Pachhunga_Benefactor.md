# Pachhunga (The Benefactor)

**Tags:** #biography #education #philanthropy

**Pachhunga** was a wealthy Mizo businessman and philanthropist of the early 20th century. He is primarily remembered for donating the land and initial funds for the establishment of **[[Pachhunga_University_College]]**, the first higher education institution in Mizoram.
