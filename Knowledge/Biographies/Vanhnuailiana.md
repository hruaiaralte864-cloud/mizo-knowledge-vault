# Vanhnuailiana (The Great Chief)

**Tags:** #biography #history

**Vanhnuailiana** was one of the most powerful and storied Sailo chiefs of the mid-19th century. He ruled over a vast territory in central Mizoram.

## Legacy of Power
He was known for his immense physical strength and his ability to command thousands of warriors. During his reign, he successfully defended his territory against numerous raids from eastern tribes and was respected even by the early British explorers who heard of his fame.

## Death and Memorial
Vanhnuailiana died in 1872. His death was a major event in Mizo history, and several poems and songs were composed in his honour. He represents the pinnacle of the [[Lal_System|Mizo chieftainship]] before the colonial transformation began.
