# Rev. Liangkhaia (The Mizo Historian)



**Rev. Liangkhaia** (1885–1979) was the first Mizo to write a comprehensive history of his people.

## Mizo Chanchin
His book **'Mizo Chanchin'** (published in 1938) is the most cited work in Mizo historiography. It bridged the gap between oral tradition and written scholarship. He also served as a chief (Lal) before resigning to become a pastor, giving him a unique dual perspective on Mizo society.
