# L. Biakliana (1895–1945)



**Lalthangliana Biakliana** is considered the father of modern Mizo fiction. His novel **Hawtinkhum** (1938), modelled on the style of Western missionary-approved literature, is celebrated as the first substantial Mizo prose novel.

He also compiled and preserved early oral folk stories, giving them literary form and helping anchor the nascent [[Mizo_Alphabet|Mizo literary tradition]] at a formative moment.
