# Lalduhoma — Chief Minister (2023–)



**Lalduhoma** is a former Indian Police Service (IPS) officer turned politician who became the Chief Minister of Mizoram in December 2023 after his party, the **[[Zoram_Peoples_Movement|Zoram People's Movement (ZPM)]]**, won a landslide electoral victory.

## Early Career
He served as an IPS officer and was once selected as a personal security officer (PSO) for Prime Minister Rajiv Gandhi. He later resigned from service and entered Mizoram politics.

## Political Journey
He was a member of the initial Congress party before founding the ZPM as a clean-slate, anti-corruption movement that resonated particularly strongly with younger Mizo voters.

## Significance
His 2023 election victory ended the political duopoly of the MNF and Congress that had defined Mizoram since statehood, representing a new generation demanding accountability in governance.
