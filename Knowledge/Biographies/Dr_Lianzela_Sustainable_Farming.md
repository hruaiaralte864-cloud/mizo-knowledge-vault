# Dr. Lianzela: Pioneer of Sustainable Mizo Farming

**Tags:** #biography #agriculture #science

**Dr. Lianzela** is a prominent agricultural scientist and academician who has revolutionized the understanding of sustainable land use in the Mizo hills.

## Scientific Reform of Jhum
Recognizing the ecological strain of traditional **[[Jhum_Cultivation]]** given the rising population, Dr. Lianzela advocated for a more scientific approach to "shifting cultivation." He introduced the concept of "Sloping Agricultural Land Technology" (SALT), which helps prevent soil erosion on steep Mizo hillsides.

## Academic Leadership
As a senior professor at **[[Mizoram_University_MZU]]**, he mentored generations of agriculturalists who are now leading the state's transition toward organic farming. His research paper on the **[[Economic_History_of_Mizoram_Agriculture]]** remains a foundational text for policymakers.

## Global Recognition
Dr. Lianzela has represented Mizoram in various international forums, bringing global attention to the unique agricultural challenges of the Himalayan foothills. He is a strong advocate for preserving the **[[Flora_of_Mizoram]]** through community-led conservation.
