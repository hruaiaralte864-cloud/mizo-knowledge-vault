﻿# Biography: Zampuimanga



## 1. Profile Summary
- **Name**: Zampuimanga
- **Era**: 20th Century
- **Title/Role**: Pasaltha / Protector
- **Primary Region**: Seling and surrounding villages

## 2. Historical Achievement
Famous for protecting villages from man-eating tigers.

## 3. Cultural Impact
Zampuimanga remains a symbol of **[[Tlawmngaihna]]** and the resilient spirit of the Zo people. Their life is often taught as a pillar of **[[Mizo_Life_and_Tradition]]**.

## 4. Legacy
Memorialized in contemporary Mizo society through songs, school curriculum, and the **[[Young_Mizo_Association]]** honors.
