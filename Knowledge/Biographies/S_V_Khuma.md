﻿# Biography: S V Khuma



## 1. Profile Summary
- **Name**: S V Khuma
- **Era**: Early 20th Century
- **Title/Role**: Educationist
- **Primary Region**: Lunglei

## 2. Historical Achievement
One of the first Mizo graduates who laid the foundation for modern schools.

## 3. Cultural Impact
S V Khuma remains a symbol of **[[Tlawmngaihna]]** and the resilient spirit of the Zo people. Their life is often taught as a pillar of **[[Mizo_Life_and_Tradition]]**.

## 4. Legacy
Memorialized in contemporary Mizo society through songs, school curriculum, and the **[[Young_Mizo_Association]]** honors.
