# Lalremsiami (born 1999)

**Tags:** #biography #sports #hockey

**Lalremsiami** is an Indian field hockey player from Mizoram who plays as a forward for the Indian women's national hockey team. She is the most celebrated Mizo sportswoman.

## Career Highlights
- Made senior international debut aged 18.
- **2018 Women's Hockey World Cup** (London) — her powerful performances in India's run to the quarter-finals brought her to global attention.
- Named **Rising Star of the Year** at the Hockey India Awards.
- **2020 Tokyo Olympics** — though India narrowly missed the medal, Lalremsiami's play was widely praised.
- **2022 Birmingham Commonwealth Games** — Bronze Medal with India.

## Impact on Mizoram
Lalremsiami's rise has inspired an enormous wave of young Mizo girls into field hockey, a sport previously dominated by the flat-land states. She is a parallel in women's sports to what [[Jeje_Lalpekhlua]] represents for men's football.
