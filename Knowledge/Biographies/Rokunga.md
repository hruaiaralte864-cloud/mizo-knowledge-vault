# Rokunga (1914–1969)

**Tags:** #biography #literature #music

**Rokunga** (full name Chuauthuama) is worshipped as the poet laureate of the Mizo people — the man who gave the nation its emotional and spiritual vocabulary in the modern era.

Active during a time of the most dramatic social transformation in Mizo history, Rokunga blended traditional oral poetic rhythms with modern lyrical forms to create songs of extraordinary power.

## His Themes
- Patriotic love for the Mizo homeland (*Zoram*).
- The profound beauty of the Mizo hills, rivers, seasons, and mist.
- The pain of separation and the longing for return.
- Christian devotion woven organically with Mizo identity.

He composed well over 150 songs, many of which are sung in churches, schools, and homes across Mizoram to this day. His patriotic anthem *Ka Lung Thlak* is particularly revered.
