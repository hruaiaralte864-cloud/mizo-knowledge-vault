# Biography: Thanga Hla Phuah Thiam

**Tags:** #history #biography #leader #Pasaltha #culture #Mizoram #research

## 1. Profile Summary
- **Name**: Thanga Hla Phuah Thiam
- **Era**: Early 20th Century
- **Title/Role**: Composer
- **Primary Region**: Aizawl

## 2. Historical Achievement
One of the first to blend Mizo philosophy with modern melody.

## 3. Cultural Impact
Thanga Hla Phuah Thiam remains a symbol of **[[Tlawmngaihna]]** and the resilient spirit of the Zo people. Their life is often taught as a pillar of **[[Mizo_Life_and_Tradition]]**.

## 4. Legacy
Memorialized in contemporary Mizo society through songs, school curriculum, and the **[[Young_Mizo_Association]]** honors.
