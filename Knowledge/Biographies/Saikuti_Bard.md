# Saikuti (The Legendary Poetess)



**Saikuti** (reigned 19th century) is one of the few women in pre-colonial Mizo history whose songs and poems have been fully preserved.

## Theme of Works
Her songs were often biting social commentaries on the pride of chiefs and the hardships of the common Mizo people. She is considered the "grandmother" of Mizo female literature.
