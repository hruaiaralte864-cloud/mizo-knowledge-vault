﻿# Biography: Taitesena



## 1. Profile Summary
- **Name**: Taitesena
- **Era**: Late 19th Century
- **Title/Role**: Pasaltha
- **Primary Region**: Western Mizo hills

## 2. Historical Achievement
Known for extreme courage and selfless protection of the weak.

## 3. Cultural Impact
Taitesena remains a symbol of **[[Tlawmngaihna]]** and the resilient spirit of the Zo people. Their life is often taught as a pillar of **[[Mizo_Life_and_Tradition]]**.

## 4. Legacy
Memorialized in contemporary Mizo society through songs, school curriculum, and the **[[Young_Mizo_Association]]** honors.
