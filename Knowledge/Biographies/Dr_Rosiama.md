# Dr. Rosiama (Medical Pioneer)

**Tags:** #biography #education #science

**Dr. Rosiama** was one of the first Mizo medical doctors. In a time when traditional medicine was the only option, he and a handful of other pioneers introduced modern Western medicine to the hills. He was a champion of public health and was instrumental in the early campaigns to eradicate malaria and cholera in Mizoram.
