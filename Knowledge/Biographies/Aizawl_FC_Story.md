# Aizawl FC — The Miracle Club



**Aizawl FC** is a professional football club from [[Aizawl_District|Aizawl]] that achieved one of the most extraordinary upsets in Asian football history.

## The 2016–17 I-League Miracle
In the **2016–17 I-League season**, Aizawl FC — a tiny club with a shoestring budget, playing in a converted hill-stadium, largely composed of local Mizo players — defeated established, wealthy clubs from Kolkata, Mumbai, and Pune to win the **I-League National Championship**.

They were provisionally suspended mid-season over an administrative issue, lost three matches on paper, but were reinstated and won the title anyway on points — defying every financial and geographic handicap.

## Global Recognition
The story attracted global media coverage — from the BBC to ESPN — as the ultimate underdog story. It demonstrated that [[Mizo_Football_Culture|Mizoram's football passion]] had real, world-competitive quality.
