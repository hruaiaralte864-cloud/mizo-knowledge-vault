# Pu Ziona (Leader of the Chana Pawl)

**Tags:** #biography #religion #society

**Pu Ziona** was the leader of the 'Chana Pawl' sect in Baktawng. He achieved global fame for his massive family and his strictly disciplined, communal lifestyle that turned his village into a model of self-sufficiency.
