# Blue Corn (Mizo Rock Band)



**Blue Corn** is one of the pioneering Rock bands in Mizoram, influential in the late 1990s and early 2000s. They were known for their meaningful lyrics and high production quality, setting a benchmark for the Mizo indie rock scene.
