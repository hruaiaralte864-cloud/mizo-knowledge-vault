# Biography: Vanapa

**Tags:** #history #biography #leader #Pasaltha #culture #Mizoram #research

## 1. Profile Summary
- **Name**: Vanapa
- **Era**: 19th Century
- **Title/Role**: Pasaltha / General
- **Primary Region**: Aizawl/Selesih

## 2. Historical Achievement
Master tactician during the tribal wars.

## 3. Cultural Impact
Vanapa remains a symbol of **[[Tlawmngaihna]]** and the resilient spirit of the Zo people. Their life is often taught as a pillar of **[[Mizo_Life_and_Tradition]]**.

## 4. Legacy
Memorialized in contemporary Mizo society through songs, school curriculum, and the **[[Young_Mizo_Association]]** honors.
