# Lalduhawma (ZPM Leader)



**Lalduhawma** is a former IPS officer who served in the security detail of Prime Minister Indira Gandhi and later transitioned into Mizo politics. He is the founder of the **[[Zoram_Peoples_Movement|Zoram People's Movement (ZPM)]]**, which has recently emerged as a major political force in the state, challenging the traditional MNF-Congress binary.
