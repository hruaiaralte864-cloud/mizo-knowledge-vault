# G-Zual (Mizo Idol Star)

**Tags:** #biography #music

**G-Zual** (Malsawmtluangi Gualnam) rose to fame through the **Mizo Idol** competition. She is a powerhouse vocalist known for her ability to sing both soulful ballads and high-energy pop songs. She has become one of the most sought-after performers for Mizo cultural events worldwide.
