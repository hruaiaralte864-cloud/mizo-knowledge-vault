# B. Lalthangliana (The Historian)

**Tags:** #biography #historian #intellectual

**B. Lalthangliana** is one of the most respected modern historians of Mizoram. His work focus on the pre-colonial migration of the Mizo people and the mapping of ancient village sites across North West Burma and Mizoram. His books are considered standard reference texts for Mizo history.
