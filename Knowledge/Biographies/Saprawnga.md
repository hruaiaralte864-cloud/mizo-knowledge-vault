# Saprawnga (Statesman)

**Tags:** #biography #politics

**Saprawnga** was a founding member of the Mizo Union and served as a member of the Lushai Hills Advisory Council. He was one of the architects of the **[[Sixth_Schedule]]** as applied to Mizoram and dedicated his life to the administrative development of the state.
