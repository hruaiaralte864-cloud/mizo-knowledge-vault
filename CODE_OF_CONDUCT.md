# Mizo Knowledge Vault: Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
As contributors and maintainers of the Mizo Knowledge Vault, we pledge to prioritize the accuracy, dignity, and cultural integrity of our collective data. We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all, regardless of clan, religion, or background.

## Our Standards
*   **Accuracy First**: All research data should be verified against primary sources or oral traditions from elders.
*   **Cultural Respect**: Treat the history and traditions of every Mizo sub-tribe with equal weight and dignity.
*   **Collaborative Spirit**: Focus on building a "Digital Monument" together.
*   **Open Access**: Maintain the data in open, portable formats (Markdown) so it remains free and accessible for all.

## Responsibility
Repository maintainers are responsible for clarifying the standards of acceptable behavior and are expected to take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any instances of unacceptable behavior.
